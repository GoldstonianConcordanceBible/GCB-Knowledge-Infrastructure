# Portfolio Review Procedure
## SydTek University

This procedure defines how student portfolios are reviewed.

---

## Purpose

Portfolio review confirms that a student can present a coherent body of work showing growth, integration, and readiness.

---

## Review Areas

Portfolio review may examine:

- variety of artifacts
- quality of organization
- technical quality
- communication quality
- reflection quality
- evidence of progression
- readiness for next-step work

---

## Public and Private Materials

Portfolio review may include both public and private materials.

Where private materials are included, authorized academic reviewers must still be able to inspect them.

---

## Review Sequence

### Step 1 — Portfolio Submission
Student submits the portfolio structure and required materials.

### Step 2 — Evidence Verification
Reviewer confirms that the materials are sufficiently documented and reviewable.

### Step 3 — Quality Review
Reviewer evaluates the portfolio against program expectations.

### Step 4 — Feedback and Decision
Reviewer records strengths, gaps, and final status.

---

## Documentation

Portfolio review results should be documented and retained according to institutional evidence practices.
