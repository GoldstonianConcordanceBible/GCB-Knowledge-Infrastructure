# Course Launch Checklist
## SydTek University

This checklist supports consistent course launch preparation.

---

## Before the Term Begins

Confirm that the following are ready:

- syllabus finalized
- course shell finalized
- major assignments identified
- rubrics prepared
- repository expectations documented
- evidence requirements documented
- required software or platforms identified
- student onboarding materials prepared

---

## GitHub-Based Course Preparation

If the course uses GitHub-based evidence, confirm that:

- repository structure guidance is available
- naming conventions are defined
- private repository review process is clear
- milestone dates are defined
- evidence dossier expectations are documented
- alternative access plans exist if students need support

---

## Open Discussion Preparation

If the course uses Open Discussion Pedagogy, confirm that:

- prompts are prepared
- evidence-linking rules are clear
- moderation expectations are documented
- equivalent rigorous pathway is available where needed
- preservation plan is defined

---

## Faculty Readiness

Confirm that the instructor has:

- reviewed the syllabus
- reviewed the rubric set
- reviewed repository review procedures
- reviewed academic integrity expectations
- reviewed audit and evidence preservation expectations

---

## Launch Confirmation

A course should be considered launch-ready when:

- all required documents are in place
- assessment expectations are clear
- student evidence pathways are defined
- faculty reviewer access process is clear
