# Capstone Review Procedure
## SydTek University

This procedure defines how capstone projects are reviewed.

---

## Purpose

The capstone review confirms that a student can integrate program learning into a final coherent project.

---

## Review Areas

Capstone review should examine:

- project scope
- technical quality
- governance or systems logic where applicable
- communication quality
- documentation quality
- evidence quality
- milestone progression
- final deliverable quality

---

## GitHub-Based Review

Where GitHub or another version-controlled environment is used, reviewers should inspect:

- repository structure
- milestone progression
- evidence of authorship
- final state of deliverables
- supporting documentation

---

## Review Sequence

### Step 1 — Proposal Review
Confirm project scope and feasibility.

### Step 2 — Midpoint Review
Confirm progression, evidence quality, and milestone completion.

### Step 3 — Final Artifact Review
Review the final project, report, repository evidence, and presentation.

### Step 4 — Final Decision
Record whether the capstone meets expectations, requires revision, or does not meet the standard.

---

## Documentation

Capstone review should generate written records suitable for program review and future audit preparation.
