# Disability Accommodations Policy
## SydTek University

SydTek University seeks to provide reasonable access to learning for students with documented needs.

---

## General Principle

Students requesting accommodations should be provided a clear process for disclosure, documentation, and support planning.

---

## Typical Accommodation Areas

Possible accommodations may include:

- testing adjustments
- accessible materials
- participation flexibility
- note-taking support
- assignment accommodations where appropriate

---

## Confidentiality

Accommodation-related information should be handled with appropriate privacy and discretion.
