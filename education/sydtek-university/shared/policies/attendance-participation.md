# Attendance and Participation Policy
## SydTek University

Attendance and participation are important parts of the learning process.

---

## General Principle

Students are expected to engage consistently with course activities, discussions, labs, and assignments.

---

## Forms of Participation

Participation may include:

- live session attendance
- discussion contributions
- lab completion
- project updates
- peer collaboration where authorized

---

## Flexibility

Course formats may vary across:

- in-person delivery
- online asynchronous delivery
- hybrid delivery
- cohort-based delivery

Attendance expectations should be stated in each course syllabus.
