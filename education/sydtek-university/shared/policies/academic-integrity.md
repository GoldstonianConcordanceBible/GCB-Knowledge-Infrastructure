# Academic Integrity Policy
## SydTek University

SydTek University expects students to complete academic work honestly and responsibly.

---

## Integrity Expectations

Students are expected to:

- submit original work unless collaboration is authorized
- properly cite sources
- accurately represent their contributions
- avoid plagiarism, fabrication, and misrepresentation

---

## AI and Tool Use

Use of AI tools, coding assistants, and automation systems must follow course-specific instructions.

Students may be required to disclose:

- what tools were used
- how they were used
- what parts of the work were human-authored

---

## Violations

Possible violations include:

- plagiarism
- unauthorized collaboration
- falsified submissions
- deceptive use of AI tools
- submission of work not created by the student as required

---

## Response

Academic integrity concerns should be documented and reviewed through the academic office or designated faculty process.
