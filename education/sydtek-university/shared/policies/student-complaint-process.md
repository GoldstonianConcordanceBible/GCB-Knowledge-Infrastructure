# Student Complaint Process
## SydTek University

SydTek University is committed to addressing student concerns in a fair and documented manner.

---

## Purpose

This process provides a pathway for students to raise academic or administrative concerns.

---

## General Steps

1. The student first attempts informal resolution with the relevant instructor or office.
2. If unresolved, the student submits a written complaint.
3. The complaint is reviewed by the appropriate academic or administrative authority.
4. A response is documented and communicated.
5. Appeals may be directed to the next level of institutional review where applicable.

---

## Documentation

Complaints and resolutions should be documented for institutional quality and accountability.
