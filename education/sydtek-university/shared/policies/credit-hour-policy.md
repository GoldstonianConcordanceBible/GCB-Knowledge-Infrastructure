# Credit Hour Policy
## SydTek University

This document provides a day-zero framework for defining academic credit.

---

## General Definition

One semester credit hour typically represents an amount of learning that includes direct instruction and additional student work outside of class.

---

## Typical Undergraduate Standard

A three-credit course normally includes a combination of:

- direct instructional time
- guided learning activity
- independent study
- assignment completion
- project or lab work

---

## Documentation Requirement

Each course syllabus should clearly indicate:

- credit hours
- expected learning activities
- major assignments
- approximate workload expectations
