# Open Discussion Pedagogy
## SydTek University Policy and Canon Summary

Open Discussion Pedagogy is a foundational pedagogical framework for SydTek University in the AI era.

It establishes that public, evidence-linked discourse can serve as a legitimate, structured, and assessable site of learning when governed by institutional standards.

## Foundational Thesis

Learning should be:

- visible where appropriate
- evidence-linked
- auditable
- critique-responsive
- preservable as academic evidence

Students should not only consume information or submit disposable assignments.

They should learn to:

- make claims responsibly
- ground those claims in evidence
- respond to critique
- revise with discipline
- produce durable intellectual artifacts

## Six Essentials

Open Discussion Pedagogy is built on six essentials:

1. public or inspectable discourse arena
2. carefully engineered prompts
3. evidence-linking standards
4. conduct governance
5. rubric-based assessment
6. preservation systems for academic records

## SydTek Interpretation Layer

Within SydTek University, the pedagogical sequence is aligned with:

Mirror → Water → Fire

Mirror:
faithful representation of text, source, claim, or problem

Water:
contextual interpretation, cleansing, comparison, and disciplined reasoning

Fire:
refinement through critique, synthesis, judgment, and integrated conclusion

## Academic Legitimacy

Public participation alone does not qualify as education.

The model becomes academically legitimate through:

- defined learning outcomes
- faculty oversight
- documented prompts
- evidence-linked responses
- rubrics
- preserved records
- consistent assessment

## Evidence Instruments

SydTek University may use:

- evidence dossiers
- audit packets
- GitHub repositories
- snapshots
- reflections
- rubric sheets
- moderation records
- exemplar anchors

## Privacy and Ethics

Open Discussion Pedagogy must never become forced exposure.

Students must be provided with equivalent rigorous pathways when public visibility creates legitimate concerns related to:

- age
- safety
- privacy
- accessibility
- family preference
- platform limitations

## Canonical Role

For SydTek University, Open Discussion Pedagogy serves as a foundational source for:

- pedagogy layer
- accreditation layer
- governance layer
- institutional identity
