# Evidence Retention Schedule
## SydTek University

This document provides a day-zero framework for retaining academic evidence related to GitHub-based learning.

## Purpose

To preserve enough academic evidence to support grading, appeals, quality review, and accreditation-oriented documentation.

## Evidence Types

Retention may include:

- repository links
- snapshots or exports
- selected commit histories
- evidence dossiers
- audit packets
- rubric sheets
- instructor feedback
- capstone records

## Public and Private Repositories

Evidence may originate from public or private repositories.

When private repositories are used, the institution should preserve enough authorized documentation to support later review.

## Principle

The institution retains academic evidence because learning must remain reviewable even when repository visibility changes later.
