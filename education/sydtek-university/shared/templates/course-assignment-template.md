## GitHub Evidence Statement

For GitHub-based assignments, academic credit depends on verifiable documented work.

Students must provide repository evidence sufficient for authorized academic review. Repositories may be public or private unless the assignment explicitly requires public engagement.

Required evidence may include:

- commit history
- issue history
- pull requests
- tagged releases
- milestone notes
- artifact snapshots

---

## Public Engagement and Equivalent Pathway

If this assignment includes public posting, peer review, or open discussion, an equivalent rigorous pathway must be available where appropriate.

The academic requirement is documented learning, critique engagement, and revision quality, not mandatory exposure.

---

## Mirror → Water → Fire Prompt Structure

Mirror  
State the source, claim, or problem accurately.

Water  
Contextualize, compare, interpret, and support with evidence.

Fire  
Refine, critique, synthesize, and conclude responsibly.
