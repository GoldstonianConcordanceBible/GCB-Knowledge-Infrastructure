## Source Selection Rule

Course readings should be selected based on:

- relevance to course outcomes
- curricular level
- academic appropriateness
- source clarity
- documentation quality

---

## Public Profile References

Public profile pages may be used for author discovery, credibility, and source navigation, but they do not replace the need for direct reading assignments when formal scholarship is required.
