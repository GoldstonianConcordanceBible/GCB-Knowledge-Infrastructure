## Criteria

### Mirror: Representation Accuracy
The student represents the source, claim, or problem faithfully and clearly.

### Water: Context and Evidence
The student interprets the material with appropriate context and supporting evidence.

### Fire: Critique and Synthesis
The student demonstrates refinement, critique engagement, and thoughtful conclusion.

### Documentation Integrity
The student provides complete, organized, and reviewable evidence.

### GitHub Evidence Quality
Where applicable, the student provides verifiable repository evidence showing authorship, progression, and milestone completion.

### Communication Quality
The student communicates clearly in written, oral, visual, or technical form.
