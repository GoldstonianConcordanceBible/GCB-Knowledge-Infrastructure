# Major Core Overview
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

The major core provides the disciplinary spine of the undergraduate degree.

Students progress from foundational understanding to systems analysis, applied design, governance reasoning, risk awareness, and capstone integration.

---

## Major Core Design Logic

The sequence is structured to move through:

1. conceptual introduction
2. historical and systems context
3. infrastructure and architecture
4. identity and ownership
5. execution logic
6. economic design
7. governance design
8. security awareness
9. infrastructure integration
10. capstone synthesis

---

## Pedagogical Alignment

Courses in the major core may align with Open Discussion Pedagogy and the Goldstonian teaching method:

Mirror  
accurate representation of concepts, systems, claims, and sources

Water  
contextualization, comparison, interpretation, and disciplined revision

Fire  
critique, refinement, synthesis, and applied conclusion

---

## Academic Evidence Principle

Where GitHub-based evidence is required, students earn credit through verifiable documented work rather than visibility alone.

Public and private repositories are both acceptable when faculty can inspect the required evidence and equivalent rigor is preserved.
