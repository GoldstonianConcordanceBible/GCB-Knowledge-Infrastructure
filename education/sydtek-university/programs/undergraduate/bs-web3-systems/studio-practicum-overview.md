# Studio and Practicum Overview
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

The studio and practicum layer provides applied, integrative, and professionally oriented learning experiences.

---

## Purpose

These courses help students:

- apply knowledge in realistic settings
- integrate multiple parts of the curriculum
- produce portfolio-ready artifacts
- document progression over time
- prepare for capstone and next-step work

---

## Studio / Practicum Components

- WEB3-420 Applied Web3 Studio
- WEB3-430 Governance Lab
- Internship / Applied Experience

---

## Academic Evidence Principle

These courses are especially likely to use GitHub-based evidence, project logs, evidence dossiers, and milestone reviews.

Academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes.

Repository visibility may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect required evidence.

---

## Public and Private Pathways

Where studios or labs include public discourse, peer review, or publication-facing activities, equivalent rigorous private pathways must remain available where appropriate.
