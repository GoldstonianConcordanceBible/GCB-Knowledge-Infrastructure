# Course Load Guidance
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document provides day-zero guidance on student course load planning.

---

## Purpose

Course load guidance supports healthy pacing, successful progression, and realistic workload planning.

---

## General Guidance

A standard full-time load is typically built around a balanced semester schedule.

Students taking multiple technical or GitHub-intensive courses in the same term may need advising support in planning workload.

---

## Heavy Documentation Terms

Terms including several of the following at once may require careful planning:

- programming-heavy courses
- GitHub-based studio work
- research writing
- capstone preparation
- internship or practicum activity

---

## Advising Note

Students should be encouraged to consider:

- technical readiness
- writing readiness
- family obligations
- employment obligations
- access to reliable devices and connectivity
- time needed for documented evidence work
