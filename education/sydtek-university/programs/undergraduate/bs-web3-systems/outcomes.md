# Program Learning Outcomes
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

The following outcomes define what graduates of this program are expected to know and do.

---

## PLO-1 Foundational Knowledge

Graduates will explain major concepts, terminology, and historical developments related to Web3 systems, blockchain infrastructure, decentralized applications, and digital ownership.

## PLO-2 Systems Analysis

Graduates will analyze the structure and behavior of distributed systems, blockchain architectures, smart contracts, wallets, and decentralized governance environments.

## PLO-3 Applied Design

Graduates will design conceptual and technical models for decentralized applications, token economies, governance systems, and interoperable Web3 infrastructures.

## PLO-4 Risk and Security Awareness

Graduates will evaluate risks, vulnerabilities, compliance challenges, and security considerations in decentralized technological systems.

## PLO-5 Communication and Professional Practice

Graduates will communicate technical ideas effectively using written, oral, visual, and repository-based formats appropriate to academic and professional audiences.

## PLO-6 Integrative Project Development

Graduates will produce a capstone-level project demonstrating the ability to synthesize architecture, incentives, governance, security, and communication into a coherent Web3 solution.
