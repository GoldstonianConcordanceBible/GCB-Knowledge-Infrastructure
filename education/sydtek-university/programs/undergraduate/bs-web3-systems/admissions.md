# Admissions Framework
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

This document provides a day-zero admissions framework for program entry.

---

## Standard Admission Requirements

Applicants should demonstrate readiness for undergraduate study through one or more of the following:

- high school diploma or equivalent
- homeschool completion documentation
- prior college coursework
- equivalent international secondary credentials

---

## Recommended Preparation

Recommended entering preparation includes:

- basic algebra
- introductory writing ability
- basic digital literacy
- interest in technology and systems thinking

---

## Alternative Pathway Admissions

Applicants with nontraditional backgrounds may be considered through:

- portfolio review
- prior learning documentation
- interview process
- evidence of technical self-study
- professional experience

---

## Transfer Credit Consideration

Transfer credit may be evaluated for:

- general education courses
- introductory computing coursework
- mathematics courses
- communication courses

Transfer credit decisions should be documented by the academic office.

---

## Language of Instruction

The primary language of instruction is English unless otherwise designated by SydTek University.

---

## Readiness Guidance

Students entering with limited technical background may be advised to begin with:

- preparatory math support
- writing support
- introductory programming support
