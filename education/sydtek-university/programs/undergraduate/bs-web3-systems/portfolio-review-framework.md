# Portfolio Review Framework
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document defines the portfolio review requirement for the degree.

---

## Purpose

The portfolio review confirms that the student can present a coherent body of academic and technical work demonstrating growth, integration, and readiness.

---

## Portfolio Components

A portfolio may include:

- selected course artifacts
- GitHub repositories or approved equivalents
- written documentation
- design diagrams
- research work
- presentations
- capstone materials
- reflection statements

---

## Review Goals

Portfolio review should assess whether the student can:

- present work professionally
- demonstrate learning across multiple areas
- explain design and reasoning choices
- show progression over time
- organize evidence clearly

---

## Public and Private Materials

Portfolio contents may include public or private materials.

Where private materials are included, authorized academic reviewers must still be able to inspect the evidence.

---

## Evaluation Areas

Portfolio review may consider:

- coherence
- technical quality
- communication quality
- documentation quality
- integrative learning
- readiness for next-step work
