# Student Progression Standards
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document provides day-zero progression expectations for students in the program.

---

## Purpose

Progression standards help ensure that students are prepared for increasingly advanced coursework.

---

## Progression Principles

Students should progress from:

- foundational understanding
- technical support competence
- systems analysis
- applied design
- integrated synthesis

---

## Advancement Expectations

Students should normally complete prerequisite coursework before enrolling in more advanced major-core courses.

Program leadership may require additional support or advising when students show gaps in:

- writing
- programming
- mathematics
- research skill
- documentation quality

---

## GitHub-Based Readiness

For courses using GitHub-based evidence, students should be prepared to:

- organize repositories clearly
- preserve work over time
- document changes
- submit reviewable artifacts
- respond to feedback and revise

---

## Intervention Principle

Students who struggle with progression expectations may be directed toward:

- tutoring
- writing support
- technical bootstrapping
- advising
- pacing adjustments
