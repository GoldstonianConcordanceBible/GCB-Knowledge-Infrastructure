# Faculty Model
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

This document outlines a day-zero faculty staffing model for program delivery.

---

## Faculty Roles

### Program Lead
Responsible for:

- curriculum oversight
- assessment coordination
- course sequencing
- program review

### Core Faculty
Responsible for:

- major core instruction
- assignment design
- student mentoring
- portfolio review

### Support Faculty
Responsible for:

- mathematics
- writing
- programming
- cybersecurity
- research support

### Adjunct or Practitioner Contributors
Responsible for:

- specialized topics
- studio teaching
- industry case studies
- guest instruction

---

## Faculty Qualification Principle

Faculty teaching within the program should demonstrate relevant preparation through one or more of the following:

- earned academic credentials
- professional experience
- research or publication record
- documented technical expertise
- teaching effectiveness

---

## Faculty Review Areas

Faculty performance may be reviewed through:

- course delivery quality
- student feedback
- assessment participation
- curriculum contribution
- mentorship and advising
