# ENG-101 Assignments
## Writing for Technical Systems

## Assignment 1
### Precision Definition Memo

Students define a technical or academic term in clear language for a non-expert audience.

---

## Assignment 2
### Source Summary Exercise

Students summarize a source accurately without distortion.

---

## Assignment 3
### Concept Explanation Note

Students explain one system-related concept step by step.

---

## Assignment 4
### Comparative Source Memo

Students compare two related sources, ideas, or definitions.

---

## Assignment 5
### Technical Audience Shift Exercise

Students rewrite the same explanation for two different audiences.

---

## Assignment 6
### Midterm Writing Portfolio Checkpoint

Students submit revised versions of earlier writing with a short reflection on growth.

---

## Assignment 7
### Source Integration Exercise

Students integrate source material into a short structured explanation with citation.

---

## Assignment 8
### Goldston Public Profile Writing Exercise

Students use one or more Goldston-linked public scholarship profiles to practice author-description, source-context writing, or credibility framing.

---

## Assignment 9
### Revision Memo

Students explain what they changed in a revised piece and why.

---

## Assignment 10
### Final Technical Writing Portfolio

Students submit a portfolio including:

- selected revised assignments
- short reflection memo
- one polished technical explanation
- one polished comparison or summary piece
