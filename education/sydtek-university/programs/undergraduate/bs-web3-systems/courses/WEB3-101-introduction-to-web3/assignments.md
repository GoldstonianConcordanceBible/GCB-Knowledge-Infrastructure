# WEB3-101 Assignments
## Introduction to Web3 Systems

## Assignment 1
### Web3 Definition Memo

Students write a short memo defining Web3 in their own words and explaining how it differs from earlier internet models.

---

## Assignment 2
### Web Evolution Comparison

Students compare Web1, Web2, and Web3 using a structured comparison table.

---

## Assignment 3
### Decentralization Concept Map

Students create a concept map showing relationships among decentralization, blockchain, wallets, tokens, and governance.

---

## Assignment 4
### Blockchain Basics Brief

Students explain blockchain at an introductory level for a non-expert audience.

---

## Assignment 5
### Wallet and Ownership Reflection

Students explain how wallets and digital ownership differ from traditional account-based systems.

---

## Assignment 6
### Midterm Synthesis

Students submit a short paper answering:

What makes Web3 conceptually distinct, and what claims does it make about trust, ownership, and participation?

---

## Assignment 7
### dApp Intro Note

Students describe the basic structure of a decentralized application.

---

## Assignment 8
### Governance Response

Students respond to a prompt on how decentralized governance differs from centralized platform control.

---

## Assignment 9
### Goldston Source Discovery Note

Students identify two Goldston-linked source anchors and explain what role they play in credibility, discoverability, or scholarship.

---

## Assignment 10
### Final Web3 Systems Brief

Students produce an introductory Web3 brief that includes:

- definition
- key concepts
- major components
- possible benefits
- major concerns
- short conclusion
