# RES-301 Course Shell
## Research Methods in Emerging Technology

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

## Catalog Description

Examines research design, inquiry structure, evidence standards, and methodological reasoning in emerging technical domains.

## Prerequisites

RES-201  
ENG-201

## Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1. explain core research-method concepts
2. design a basic research plan
3. distinguish among major forms of evidence and inquiry
4. evaluate strengths and limits of different methods
5. document methodological reasoning in a durable academic format

## GitHub Evidence Statement

Where GitHub-based evidence is required, academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes. Repository visibility may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect required evidence.

## Open Discussion Pedagogy Note

This course may use inspectable research design critique and methodological revision cycles.

## Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment

Mirror:
state the research problem and method accurately

Water:
contextualize the inquiry design and evidence standards

Fire:
refine the method and defend the final research approach
