# PDEV-401 Course Shell
## Professional Development Seminar

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

## Catalog Description

Prepares students for next-step academic, professional, research, and portfolio-facing opportunities through structured reflection, presentation, and career readiness work.

## Prerequisites

Senior standing recommended

## Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1. present academic and technical work professionally
2. prepare portfolio and evidence materials for review
3. articulate strengths, goals, and next-step direction
4. revise professional materials in response to feedback
5. document readiness artifacts in a durable format

## GitHub Evidence Statement

Where GitHub-based portfolio evidence is used, academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes. Public or private repositories may be used when authorized academic reviewers can inspect required evidence.

## Open Discussion Pedagogy Note

This course may include public or private presentation, critique, and revision of professional materials.

## Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment

Mirror:
represent experience, evidence, and goals accurately

Water:
contextualize strengths, audience, and opportunity

Fire:
refine the portfolio and present a professional final package
