# WEB3-220 Course Shell
## Wallets, Identity, and Digital Ownership

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

## Catalog Description

Explores decentralized identity, wallets, authorization, custody, signature logic, and digital ownership in blockchain-based systems.

## Prerequisites

WEB3-101

## Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1. explain how wallets function in decentralized systems
2. distinguish custody models and ownership claims
3. describe the role of signatures and authorization
4. analyze identity and access issues in Web3 systems
5. preserve evidence of learning through documented artifacts
