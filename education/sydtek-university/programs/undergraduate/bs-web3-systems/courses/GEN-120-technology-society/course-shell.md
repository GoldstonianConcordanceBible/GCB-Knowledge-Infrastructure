# GEN-120 Course Shell
## Technology and Society

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

## Catalog Description

Examines the relationship between technological systems and social life, including power, institutions, access, labor, culture, and public discourse.

## Prerequisites

None

## Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1. explain key social questions raised by technology
2. analyze how technical systems affect people and institutions
3. compare competing social interpretations of innovation
4. communicate evidence-based perspectives responsibly
5. preserve social analysis artifacts for academic review

## GitHub Evidence Statement

In GitHub-based assignments, academic credit depends on verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes. Public or private repositories may be used when authorized academic reviewers can inspect required evidence.

## Open Discussion Pedagogy Note

This course is naturally compatible with structured public or private discourse on contested technological issues.
