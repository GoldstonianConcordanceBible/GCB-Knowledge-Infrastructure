# WEB3-110 Course Shell
## Internet Evolution: Web1, Web2, and Web3

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

## Catalog Description

Examines the historical development of the internet from static publishing environments to centralized platform ecosystems and decentralized infrastructures.

## Prerequisites

None

## Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1. describe the historical phases of internet development
2. compare Web1, Web2, and Web3 structures
3. evaluate shifts in control, ownership, and participation
4. analyze technological and social consequences of platform design
5. preserve evidence-linked academic work
