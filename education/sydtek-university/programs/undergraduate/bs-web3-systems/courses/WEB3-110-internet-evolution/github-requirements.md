# WEB3-110 GitHub Requirements
## Internet Evolution: Web1, Web2, and Web3

## Core Requirement

Academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes.

Repositories may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect required evidence.

---

## Minimum Expectations

Students should preserve:

- comparison tables
- response memos
- midterm analysis
- final paper drafts
- final paper
- reflection notes where required

---

## Recommended Structure

- README.md
- comparisons/
- memos/
- midterm/
- final/
- evidence-notes/

---

## Review Principle

Credit is awarded for documented learning, revision, and completion, not repository visibility alone.
