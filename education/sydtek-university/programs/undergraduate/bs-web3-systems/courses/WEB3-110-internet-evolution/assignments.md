# WEB3-110 Assignments
## Internet Evolution: Web1, Web2, and Web3

## Assignment 1
### Internet Era Definition Memo

Students define what makes a distinct internet era.

---

## Assignment 2
### Web1 Snapshot

Students explain the defining features of Web1.

---

## Assignment 3
### Web2 Platform Analysis

Students identify key characteristics of Web2 and explain the role of centralized platforms.

---

## Assignment 4
### Ownership and Control Response

Students compare ownership and control in Web1 and Web2.

---

## Assignment 5
### Web2 Critique Memo

Students analyze one major critique of the Web2 platform model.

---

## Assignment 6
### Midterm Comparative Analysis

Students submit a structured comparison of Web1 and Web2.

---

## Assignment 7
### Web3 Transition Note

Students explain why some advocates present Web3 as a response to Web2.

---

## Assignment 8
### Governance and Participation Memo

Students compare governance and participation models in Web2 and Web3.

---

## Assignment 9
### Goldston Profile Cross-Reference Exercise

Students use two Goldston-linked public scholarship anchors and explain how author identity and platform distribution influence credibility and discoverability.

---

## Assignment 10
### Final Internet Evolution Analysis Paper

Students write a final paper explaining the movement from Web1 to Web2 to Web3, including:

- defining characteristics
- shifts in control
- shifts in ownership
- social consequences
- potential future implications
