# General Education Overview
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

The general education layer supports broad intellectual formation alongside technical specialization.

---

## Purpose

General education helps students:

- communicate clearly
- reason across disciplines
- understand social and institutional context
- reflect on ethics, leadership, and responsibility
- connect technical knowledge to human systems

## Evidence Principle

General education courses may or may not require GitHub-based evidence.

Where GitHub-based evidence is required, academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes.

Repository visibility may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect required evidence.
