# Executive Summary
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

This program is designed for the AI era and uses documented digital work as a primary evidence source for academic credit.

The degree combines:

- machine-readable books
- GitHub-based academic artifacts
- structured assessment
- public or private evidence pathways
- rubric-based evaluation
- capstone and portfolio development

The foundational curricular source for the program is:

Welcome to Web3: A Multi-Level Introduction to the Decentralized Internet

The foundational pedagogical and governance source for the broader SydTek model is:

Open Discussion Pedagogy: A PhD-Level Framework for Public, Evidence-Linked Learning

## Core Academic Principle

Students earn credit for verified academic work, not for visibility alone.

SydTek University requires documented evidence of learning through GitHub commits, linked artifacts, reflections, and instructor review.

Repositories may be public or private depending on student or parent choice, provided that authorized faculty can verify the work for assessment and audit purposes.

## Why This Matters

This model allows SydTek University to combine:

- openness where appropriate
- privacy where necessary
- academic rigor in all cases
- auditability for institutional review
- durable evidence for accreditation-oriented documentation

## Institutional Distinctive

SydTek University treats learning as evidence-linked, method-driven, and inspectable.

The university’s pedagogical model is aligned with:

Mirror → Water → Fire

Mirror:
faithful representation, accurate observation, and evidence-grounded claim formation

Water:
contextualization, interpretation, and disciplined revision

Fire:
refinement, critique, synthesis, and demonstrated mastery

## Accreditation-Ready Principle

Public learning can be accreditation-ready when it is:

- outcome-aligned
- rubric-scored
- faculty-supervised
- documented
- preserved in evidence dossiers and audit packets

Public participation is not the academic standard by itself.

The academic standard is documented, assessable learning under institutional oversight.
