# Graduation Requirements
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document defines the launch-stage graduation requirements for the undergraduate degree.

---

## Degree Completion Standard

To graduate, students must successfully complete the required curriculum, satisfy academic standards, and submit all culminating evidence required by the program.

---

## Graduation Requirements

Students must complete:

- major core requirements
- technical support core requirements
- mathematics and analytics requirements
- communication, ethics, and research requirements
- elective requirements
- internship or practicum requirement where applicable
- senior capstone requirement
- portfolio review requirement

---

## Academic Standing Requirement

Students must remain in good academic standing according to SydTek University academic policies and program expectations.

---

## Evidence Requirement

In courses and milestones designated as GitHub-based, students must provide verifiable documented academic work aligned to course outcomes.

Repository visibility may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect the required evidence.

---

## Capstone and Portfolio Requirement

Students must complete:

- a senior capstone project
- an evidence dossier where required
- a portfolio review demonstrating integrated learning across the program

---

## Final Review

Graduation approval should confirm:

- required credits completed
- required courses passed
- capstone completed
- portfolio reviewed
- institutional requirements satisfied
