# Credit Hour Framework
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document provides the degree-level credit-hour structure for the undergraduate program.

---

## Degree Credit Framework

The undergraduate degree is designed as a 120-credit bachelor's program.

120 credits

---

## Credit-Hour Documentation Principle

Each course syllabus should identify:

- number of credits
- expected workload
- major assignments
- direct instructional and guided learning expectations

---

## GitHub-Based Learning Note

Use of GitHub as an evidence environment does not alter the credit-hour standard.

Credit hours remain tied to documented student learning, faculty oversight, and course workload expectations.
