# Article Integration Framework
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document explains how article-level scholarship should be integrated into the undergraduate degree.

---

## Purpose

Articles help move the curriculum beyond introductory explanation into formal scholarly engagement.

They are especially useful in:

- writing courses
- research courses
- ethics courses
- upper-division Web3 courses
- studio and capstone preparation

---

## Article Functions in the Curriculum

Articles may be used as:

- concept-deepening readings
- case study sources
- comparative analysis sources
- research-method examples
- literature review anchors
- advanced discussion prompts

---

## Assignment Types Supported by Articles

- annotated bibliography
- source comparison memo
- article response paper
- literature map
- research brief
- concept translation exercise
- public scholarship critique

---

## Source Discovery Rule

Where instructor-assigned direct articles are not yet fully stored in course folders, course teams may use:

- Google Scholar
- ResearchGate
- PhilArchive
- arXiv
- Zenodo

as discovery layers for selecting article-linked material related to course outcomes.
