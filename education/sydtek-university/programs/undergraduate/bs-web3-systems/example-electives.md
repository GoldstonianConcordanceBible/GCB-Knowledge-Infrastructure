# Example Electives
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This file provides example elective directions that can be developed into future course shells.

---

## Example Elective Areas

### AI-310 AI Agents and Knowledge Systems
Explores agent-based systems, workflow automation, tool use, and knowledge routing.

### PUB-320 Machine-Readable Publishing
Explores structured publishing, knowledge repositories, metadata, and AI-readable text systems.

### ENT-310 Entrepreneurship in Emerging Technology
Explores venture design, innovation strategy, opportunity framing, and new-technology ecosystems.

### PHIL-340 Philosophy of Technology
Examines philosophical interpretations of technology, agency, power, and knowledge.

### DATA-330 Data Visualization for Digital Systems
Introduces visual communication of technical and analytical data.

### THEO-360 Theology of Technology
Explores theological reflection on technical systems, mediation, power, and digital life.
