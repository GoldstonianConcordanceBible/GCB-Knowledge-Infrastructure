# Support Course Catalog
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

---

## Technical Support Core

### CS-101 Programming Fundamentals
Credits: 3

Introduces foundational programming concepts including variables, logic, control flow, functions, debugging, and problem solving.

### CS-120 Web Development Foundations
Credits: 3

Introduces web development principles including HTML, CSS, JavaScript fundamentals, interface structure, and basic client-side behavior.

### CS-210 Data Structures
Credits: 3

Introduces key data structures, algorithmic thinking, storage patterns, and computational organization for technical problem solving.

### CS-240 Databases and Data Modeling
Credits: 3

Examines structured data, relational models, schemas, queries, normalization, and data organization principles.

### CS-260 API and Systems Integration
Credits: 3

Explores APIs, service communication, integration workflows, request-response models, and system interoperability.

### CYB-210 Cybersecurity Fundamentals
Credits: 3

Introduces foundational security concepts including access control, threat modeling, basic vulnerabilities, secure practices, and risk awareness.

---

## Mathematics and Analytics Core

### MATH-101 College Algebra
Credits: 3

Develops algebraic reasoning, equations, functions, and quantitative problem-solving needed for undergraduate technical study.

### MATH-210 Discrete Mathematics
Credits: 3

Introduces logic, sets, relations, combinatorics, graphs, and mathematical structures relevant to computer and information systems.

### STAT-201 Statistics for Digital Systems
Credits: 3

Introduces descriptive statistics, probability, inference, and data interpretation for technical and digital environments.

---

## Communication, Ethics, and Research Core

### ENG-101 Writing for Technical Systems
Credits: 3

Develops clear, structured, and audience-aware writing for technical, academic, and documentation contexts.

### ENG-201 Research Writing and Documentation
Credits: 3

Builds research writing skills including source integration, citation, evidence use, and formal documentation.

### COMM-101 Public Communication
Credits: 3

Develops oral communication, presentation, structured argument, and audience engagement skills.

### ETH-220 Ethics of Decentralized Technology
Credits: 3

Examines ethical questions in decentralized systems, AI, digital ownership, governance, and technological power.

### RES-201 Information Literacy and Research
Credits: 3

Introduces research strategy, source evaluation, information credibility, and evidence selection.

### RES-301 Research Methods in Emerging Technology
Credits: 3

Examines research design, inquiry structure, evidence standards, and methodological thinking in emerging technical domains.
