# Practicum Model
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

This document outlines a day-zero practicum model for applied student experience.

---

## Purpose

The practicum gives students an opportunity to apply classroom learning in a supervised environment.

Possible practicum settings include:

- GitHub-based project teams
- research labs
- decentralized governance simulations
- digital product studios
- supervised external projects

---

## Practicum Learning Goals

Students will:

- apply technical knowledge to real or simulated systems
- document work professionally
- collaborate within structured project settings
- reflect on design, risk, and communication

---

## Practicum Deliverables

Students should produce:

- project plan
- work log
- artifact portfolio
- supervisor or mentor feedback
- final reflection

---

## Evaluation Areas

Practicum evaluation may include:

- reliability
- technical contribution
- communication
- documentation quality
- reflective learning
