# Program Assessment Rubric
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure

Institution: SydTek University

This rubric provides a simple day-zero framework for reviewing major student artifacts.

---

## Rating Scale

4 = Exceeds Expectations  
3 = Meets Expectations  
2 = Approaches Expectations  
1 = Does Not Meet Expectations

---

## Criteria

### Technical Understanding
The student demonstrates accurate understanding of core concepts and systems.

### Systems Design
The student organizes components into a coherent technical or governance model.

### Risk Awareness
The student identifies security, governance, or implementation risks appropriately.

### Communication
The student communicates clearly in writing, diagrams, and presentations.

### Documentation Quality
The student provides organized, complete, and professional supporting materials.

### Integration
The student connects multiple areas of the curriculum into one coherent result.
