# Internship Framework
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

This document defines the internship framework for the undergraduate program.

---

## Purpose

The internship provides supervised applied learning in a professional, technical, research, or project environment.

---

## Internship Goals

Students should be able to:

- apply program knowledge in practice
- contribute to real or realistic technical work
- communicate professionally
- reflect on performance and growth
- document evidence of applied learning

---

## Possible Settings

Internships may occur in settings such as:

- research groups
- technical startups
- nonprofit digital initiatives
- decentralized project teams
- supervised institutional labs
- approved independent project environments

---

## Required Deliverables

Students should complete:

- internship learning agreement
- supervisor or mentor check-in
- work log
- artifact sample or project evidence
- reflective summary
- final evaluation

---

## GitHub Evidence Note

Where appropriate, internships may use GitHub or other version-controlled environments to document student contributions.

Academic credit depends on verifiable documented work, approved supervision, and outcome-aligned evaluation.

---

## Review Standard

Internships should be reviewed for:

- professionalism
- evidence quality
- relevance to learning goals
- quality of reflection
- satisfactory completion
