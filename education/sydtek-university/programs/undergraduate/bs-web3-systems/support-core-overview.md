# Support Core Overview
## BS in Web3 Systems and Decentralized Infrastructure
## SydTek University

The support core provides the programming, data, mathematics, communication, ethics, and research foundation required for success in the Web3 major core.

---

## Purpose

The support core exists to ensure that students can:

- write and understand technical systems
- work with modern web and data structures
- reason quantitatively
- communicate professionally
- conduct research responsibly
- analyze technological systems in ethical and social context

---

## Academic Evidence Principle

Where GitHub-based evidence is required in support-core courses, academic credit is awarded for verifiable documented work aligned to course outcomes.

Repository visibility may be public or private unless otherwise stated, but authorized academic reviewers must be able to inspect required evidence.
