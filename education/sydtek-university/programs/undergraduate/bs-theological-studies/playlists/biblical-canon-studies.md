Course Title
Biblical Canon Studies

Playlist Title
Biblical Canon Studies Explained | Ethiopian Canon | Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)

Description
This course explores the historical formation of the biblical canon with particular emphasis on the 81-Book Ethiopian Canon. Students examine manuscript traditions, canon development, early church debates, and theological implications through the Mirror → Water → Fire interpretive framework.
