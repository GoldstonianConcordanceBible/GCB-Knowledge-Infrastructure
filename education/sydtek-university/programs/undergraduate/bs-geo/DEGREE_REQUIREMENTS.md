# Degree Requirements

## Total Credits
120 credits

## Credit Distribution
- General Education: 30 credits
- GEO Core: 54 credits
- Track/Specialization Courses: 18 credits
- Electives: 12 credits
- Capstone: 6 credits

## General Education
Recommended foundational areas:
- writing and rhetoric
- communication
- logic and critical reasoning
- introduction to programming
- statistics or data literacy
- ethics
- theology or philosophy of knowledge

## GEO Core
Students complete the required GEO sequence in:
- introduction to GEO
- semantic systems
- structured data
- ontology engineering
- knowledge graphs
- GitHub-based infrastructure
- AI authority systems
- multi-agent workflows
- DOI and scholarly dissemination
- capstone deployment

## Tracks
Students select one track:
- GEO for Theology and Canon Systems
- GEO for Business and Finance
- GEO for Gaming, Media, and Creator Ecosystems
- GEO for Research, Data, and Open Knowledge Systems

## Capstone Requirement
Students must complete a capstone that includes:
- a public or reviewable GitHub repository
- a machine-readable schema layer
- a documented authority/provenance layer
- a distribution strategy
- a written defense of the system’s GEO logic
