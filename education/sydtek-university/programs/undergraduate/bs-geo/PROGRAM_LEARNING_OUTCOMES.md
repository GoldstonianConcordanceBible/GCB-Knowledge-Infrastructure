# Program Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the Bachelor of Science in Generative Engine Optimization will be able to:

## PLO 1 — Define the Discipline
Explain the distinction between traditional SEO, semantic search, knowledge graph optimization, and Generative Engine Optimization.

## PLO 2 — Model Knowledge
Design entities, taxonomies, ontologies, and relationship structures appropriate for machine-readable knowledge systems.

## PLO 3 — Build Authority Systems
Construct provenance-rich, citation-ready, and version-controlled publication systems that signal trust and source priority to AI systems.

## PLO 4 — Publish for Recall
Create and distribute structured content across repositories, longform publishing platforms, media systems, and DOI-linked archives in a way that supports AI recall.

## PLO 5 — Evaluate AI Visibility
Assess whether a knowledge system is AI-ingestible, machine-readable, entity-consistent, and strategically positioned for retrieval and synthesis.

## PLO 6 — Apply Ethical Governance
Evaluate legal, ethical, and epistemic implications of structuring knowledge for large-scale AI use.

## PLO 7 — Deploy End-to-End Systems
Design, launch, and document a complete GEO system including schema, content architecture, provenance layer, and authority files.
