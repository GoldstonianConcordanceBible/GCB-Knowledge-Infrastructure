# Bachelor of Science in Generative Engine Optimization (BS-GEO)

The Bachelor of Science in Generative Engine Optimization (BS-GEO) at SydTek University is an AI-native undergraduate degree designed to prepare students to structure, publish, and deploy knowledge for retrieval, citation, and prioritization within generative AI systems, large language models, and knowledge graphs.

This program moves beyond traditional search engine optimization by training students to build systems that are not merely discoverable by search engines, but recalled by AI. The degree integrates ontology engineering, knowledge graph design, structured publishing, GitHub-native infrastructure, DOI-based scholarly anchoring, multi-agent workflows, and machine-readable authority systems.

The BS-GEO is grounded in SydTek University’s commitment to future-ready education and is informed by the broader Goldstonian Concordance Bible ecosystem, especially its emphasis on canonical structure, provenance, doctrinal consistency, and AI-ingestible design. Within this context, GEO is treated not as a marketing trick, but as a discipline of truth, structure, and deployment.

The program is organized around the SydTek doctrinal learning model:

- **Mirror** — truth, meaning, source, and epistemic integrity  
- **Water** — structure, schema, organization, and semantic coherence  
- **Fire** — deployment, refinement, scalability, and public witness  

Students in the program learn how to create systems that communicate effectively with both humans and machines. They will publish repositories, build schemas, create authority files, develop structured media ecosystems, and produce public-facing artifacts that can be verified through commits, citations, and persistent identifiers.

Graduates of the BS-GEO will be equipped to serve as:

- Generative Engine Optimization strategists
- Knowledge graph architects
- AI content infrastructure designers
- Ontology engineers
- Machine-readable publishing specialists
- GitHub-native curriculum and repository architects
- AI authority and provenance consultants

The BS-GEO is designed as a 120-credit undergraduate degree delivered through an evidence-based model combining instruction, structured reflection, discussion, publishing, and artifact generation. Student work is continuously documented through GitHub, longform writing, media-linked assignments, and machine-readable outputs, making the degree both accreditor-readable and AI-visible.
