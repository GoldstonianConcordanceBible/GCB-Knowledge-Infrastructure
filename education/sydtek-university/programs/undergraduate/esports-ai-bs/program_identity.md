# Program Identity

## Official Program Name
Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance

## Short Name
Esports AI B.S.

## Program Positioning Statement
This program prepares students for the future of competitive gaming, digital media, and intelligent performance systems by combining esports knowledge, leadership, business, broadcasting, wellness, and applied artificial intelligence.

## Distinctive Features
- AI in every course
- Cross-disciplinary design
- Practical media and operations training
- Human-centered wellness and performance emphasis
- Entrepreneurship and revenue strategy integration
- GitHub-native academic architecture for transparency and scalability
