# Program Review Schedule

## Purpose
This document defines the recurring schedule for program review, assessment review, governance review, and curriculum improvement.

## Review Calendar

### Each Academic Term
- course-level assessment evidence collected
- course delivery and participation reviewed
- AI disclosure and usage practices monitored where applicable

### Annually
- program learning outcomes reviewed
- KPI dashboard updated
- curriculum committee reviews findings
- advisory input requested or reviewed
- governance documents checked for needed updates
- closing-the-loop actions documented

### Every Two to Three Years
- broader curriculum coherence review
- market and workforce relevance review
- technology and AI integration review
- elective relevance review
- faculty qualification review

### Prior to Major Program Change
- mission alignment rechecked
- course sequence and outcomes revalidated
- approval workflow completed
- evidence index updated

## Evidence of Review
Review activities should generate:
- annual review documentation
- program change logs
- assessment summaries
- closing-the-loop reports
- updated governance or curriculum files when revisions occur
