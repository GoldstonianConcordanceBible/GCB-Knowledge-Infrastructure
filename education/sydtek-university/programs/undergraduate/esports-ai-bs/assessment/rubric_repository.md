# Rubric Repository

This repository document defines standard rubric domains used across the program.

## Core Rubric Domains
- Content knowledge
- Applied skill
- Analytical reasoning
- Ethical reasoning
- Communication
- Leadership and collaboration
- AI use and disclosure
- Data interpretation
- Professional presentation

## Standard Performance Levels
- Exemplary
- Proficient
- Developing
- Beginning

## Universal AI Rubric Add-On
When AI is used in an assignment, the following additional categories may be added:
- Appropriate tool selection
- Transparency of use
- Quality control of AI output
- Critical evaluation of limitations
- Human revision and ownership

## Course-Level Adaptation
Faculty may adapt assignment-specific rubrics so long as they preserve program outcome alignment and allow consistent evidence collection.
