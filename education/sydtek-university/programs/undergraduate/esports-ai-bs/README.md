# SydTek University
## Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance

This repository contains the academic, governance, assessment, and accreditation-ready structure for the SydTek University undergraduate program in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

## Mission
To prepare students to lead in esports, gaming, digital media, and performance environments through the integration of artificial intelligence, ethical leadership, business strategy, and applied analytics.

## Program Highlights
- AI integrated across the full curriculum
- Accreditation-aligned academic structure
- Outcomes-based learning model
- Ethics, governance, and responsible AI emphasis
- GitHub-native documentation model
- Workforce, media, coaching, and venture readiness

## Degree Requirements
- 40 credits of core coursework
- 9 credits of electives
- Total: 49 semester hours

## Institutional Affiliation Statement
This repository is affiliated with SydTek University and supports its academic, curriculum, and research infrastructure. It is structured in alignment with accreditation-oriented standards for future institutional review and continuous improvement.

## Repo Structure
- governance → policies, oversight, and review processes
- program → catalog, degree requirements, and learning outcomes
- courses → course folders and syllabi
- electives → elective course folders and syllabi
- assessment → program evaluation and student learning evidence
- data → dashboards and tracking files
- mooc_integration → playlist and instructional-time tracking
- templates → reusable academic templates
