# Stackability Map

## Purpose
This document describes how the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance may connect to future certificates, graduate study, microcredentials, and workforce-aligned pathways.

## Undergraduate Stackability
The program may support stackable internal recognition in the following areas:
- Esports Operations
- AI for Digital Media
- Broadcasting and Streaming Production
- Fan Engagement and Analytics
- Gaming Entrepreneurship
- Coaching and Performance Support

## Post-Baccalaureate Pathways
Graduates may continue into future SydTek University or partner-aligned study areas such as:
- M.S. in AI Systems
- M.S. in Digital Media Strategy
- M.S. in Sports and Performance Administration
- Graduate certificate in AI Governance
- Graduate certificate in Analytics and Decision Support
- Graduate certificate in Entrepreneurship and Venture Design

## Workforce and Portfolio Stackability
The program is designed to produce artifacts that may also support:
- portfolio-based employment applications
- coaching and media credentials
- startup incubation or venture development
- creator economy and digital consulting pathways

## Design Principle
Stackability should preserve academic integrity while allowing students to build progressively from foundational coursework into advanced practice, specialization, and leadership.
