# Institutional Mission Alignment

## SydTek University Mission (Proposed)
SydTek University prepares students to lead in a rapidly evolving world through the integration of technology, ethical reasoning, applied learning, and human-centered leadership.

---

## Program Alignment

### Technology Integration
This program integrates artificial intelligence across all courses, preparing students to operate in AI-enabled environments.

### Applied Learning
Students complete real-world assignments including media production, analytics, strategy development, and performance planning.

### Ethical Leadership
Courses include ethical analysis, governance, wellness, and responsible AI usage.

### Workforce Readiness
The curriculum aligns with careers in esports, gaming, media, business, and digital strategy.

### Innovation and Entrepreneurship
Students develop business models, revenue strategies, and venture ideas within esports ecosystems.

---

## Alignment Summary
The Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance program directly supports the institutional mission through interdisciplinary learning, AI integration, ethical reasoning, and applied skill development.
