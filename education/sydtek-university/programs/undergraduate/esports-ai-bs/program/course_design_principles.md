# Course Design Principles

## Purpose
These principles guide the design of all courses in the program.

## Principles
1. Every course must support program identity and workforce relevance
2. Every course must align with one or more program learning outcomes
3. Every course must include AI integration appropriate to the subject
4. Every course must produce at least one assessable artifact
5. Every course must include ethical reasoning where relevant
6. Every course must document instructional engagement
7. Every course must support evidence collection and review
8. Every course should balance theory, practice, and reflection

## Course Quality Questions
- What should students know by the end of the course?
- What should students be able to do?
- How does AI appear in the discipline?
- What evidence will show learning?
- How does the course contribute to the full degree?
