# Satisfactory Academic Progress Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the minimum academic progress expectations for students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

## Policy Statement
Students are expected to make consistent and measurable progress toward completion of the program.

## Core Standards
Satisfactory Academic Progress should consider:
- cumulative academic performance
- successful course completion
- pace of progression through the curriculum
- continued engagement with required coursework and assessments

## Minimum Expectations
Students are expected to:
- remain in acceptable academic standing under institutional standards
- complete coursework at a pace that supports timely program completion
- meet required participation and submission expectations in enrolled courses
- satisfy academic integrity and AI disclosure expectations where applicable

## Review Timing
Academic progress should be reviewed at regular intervals, including at minimum the end of each academic term or evaluation period.

## Warning and Intervention
Students who fall below progress expectations may be placed on:
- academic warning
- academic improvement plan
- probation or equivalent institutional status

Support measures may include:
- advising
- tutoring or academic support
- schedule adjustment
- revised study planning
- technology and workflow coaching

## Failure to Meet Progress Standards
Continued failure to meet satisfactory academic progress expectations may affect continued enrollment, progression, or other institutional determinations.

## Documentation
Academic progress reviews and resulting actions should be documented and retained for institutional records.
