## Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance

The Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance is an interdisciplinary undergraduate program that prepares students for careers in competitive gaming, digital media, team performance, event operations, and gaming entrepreneurship. The curriculum integrates business, broadcasting, wellness, coaching, administration, social media, live event production, and game design with applied artificial intelligence across the full program of study.

Students develop knowledge of the esports ecosystem, industry trends, fan behavior, leadership, revenue generation, and contemporary issues affecting digital performance environments. The program emphasizes ethical AI use, analytics, operational strategy, and human-centered leadership to equip graduates for emerging roles in esports and related industries.

### Total Program Hours
49 semester hours

### Program Requirements
- 40 semester hours of core courses
- 9 semester hours of electives

### Delivery Modes
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

### Program Features
- AI integrated in every course
- Outcomes-based curriculum design
- Applied project and portfolio development
- Accreditation-oriented assessment structure
- Industry and entrepreneurship alignment
