# Records Retention Policy

## Purpose
To ensure that academic, assessment, governance, and compliance records are maintained in an organized, secure, and reviewable manner.

## Records Covered
- Syllabi
- Assignments
- Rubrics
- Program review reports
- Assessment summaries
- Evidence logs
- Curriculum committee notes
- AI compliance records
- Course sequence maps
- Enrollment and completion data

## Retention Expectations
Records should be maintained for a period sufficient to support program review, audit, and accreditation readiness. Where institutional policy is later adopted, this file should be updated to match that standard.

## Access
Records should be available to authorized institutional reviewers, program leadership, and approved auditors or accrediting reviewers when applicable.

## Format
Records may be maintained in Markdown, CSV, PDF, or approved institutional systems, provided they remain accessible and version-controlled.
