# Faculty Qualification Framework

## Purpose
This framework provides minimum expectations for faculty teaching in the Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance program.

## Core Expectations
Faculty should demonstrate one or more of the following:
- Academic preparation in a relevant discipline
- Professional experience in esports, gaming, business, media, coaching, or AI-related work
- Evidence of teaching effectiveness
- Ability to integrate AI ethically and appropriately into instruction

## Documentation Categories
- Degrees and certifications
- Professional experience
- Portfolio of relevant work
- Licenses where applicable
- Teaching evaluations
- Publications, presentations, or project leadership where applicable

## Assignment of Faculty
Faculty should be assigned to courses based on demonstrated alignment between expertise and course content.

## Continuous Development
Faculty are encouraged to participate in ongoing professional development in AI tools, digital pedagogy, ethics, and relevant industry trends.
