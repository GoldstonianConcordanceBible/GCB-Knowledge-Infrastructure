# Advisory Board Framework

## Purpose
The advisory board provides external perspective on industry relevance, workforce alignment, technology change, and strategic program direction.

## Suggested Membership
- Esports industry professional
- Gaming entrepreneur
- Broadcast/media specialist
- Business or operations leader
- AI systems advisor
- Higher education advisor
- Wellness or performance specialist

## Suggested Review Areas
- Curriculum relevance
- Emerging technology needs
- Industry tool adoption
- Internship and experiential learning pathways
- Student employability
- Entrepreneurship opportunities

## Meeting Frequency
At least annually, with additional consultation as needed.

## Documentation
Meeting notes, recommendations, and resulting actions should be summarized and retained for evidence of external input.
