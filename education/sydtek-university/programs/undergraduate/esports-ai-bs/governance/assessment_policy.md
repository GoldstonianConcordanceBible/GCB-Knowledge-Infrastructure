# Assessment Policy

## Policy Statement
All academic programs at SydTek University shall maintain a documented assessment process that measures student learning outcomes, supports continuous improvement, and produces evidence suitable for internal review, external audit, and future accreditation processes.

## Program Responsibilities
The Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance program shall:
- Maintain current program learning outcomes
- Map outcomes to courses
- Collect and store evidence of student learning
- Conduct annual review
- Document program changes based on evidence
- Preserve assessment records in an organized and reviewable format

## Faculty Responsibilities
Faculty are responsible for:
- Using aligned assignments and rubrics
- Submitting required assessment artifacts
- Participating in annual assessment conversations
- Reporting issues affecting outcome attainment
- Documenting AI-related learning and compliance where relevant

## Evidence Retention
Assessment evidence shall be retained according to institutional records expectations and preserved in the repository or approved institutional storage systems.
