# Program Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance will be able to:

## PLO 1
Analyze the esports and gaming ecosystem, including its operational, economic, social, and technological dimensions.

## PLO 2
Apply artificial intelligence tools to esports decision-making, media workflows, analytics, business operations, and performance support.

## PLO 3
Design and evaluate digital marketing, social media, and fan engagement strategies using data-informed methods.

## PLO 4
Assess player wellness, team performance, and human factors using ethical and human-centered frameworks.

## PLO 5
Produce and manage esports-related media, streaming, broadcasts, live events, and digital experiences.

## PLO 6
Develop sustainable revenue, entrepreneurship, and partnership strategies for esports and gaming organizations.

## PLO 7
Demonstrate leadership, communication, administration, and ethical reasoning in fast-changing digital environments.

## PLO 8
Interpret data, dashboards, and evidence to support planning, assessment, and continuous improvement in esports organizations.
