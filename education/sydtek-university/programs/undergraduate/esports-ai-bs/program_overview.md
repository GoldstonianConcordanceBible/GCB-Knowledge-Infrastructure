# Program Overview

## Degree Title
Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance

## Award
Bachelor of Science

## Academic Level
Undergraduate

## Department
SydTek University

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported delivery

## Program Length
49 semester hours

## Program Summary
The Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance prepares students for leadership roles across esports, gaming, digital media, team operations, coaching, live event production, entrepreneurship, and AI-enabled performance environments. The program combines esports industry knowledge with business, wellness, broadcasting, game design, digital strategy, and applied artificial intelligence.

Students learn to analyze competitive ecosystems, manage organizations, support player development, produce digital media, interpret data, and apply AI tools responsibly in operational and strategic settings. The program is designed to produce graduates who are future-ready, ethically grounded, and capable of building value in both traditional and emerging performance environments.

## Program Purpose
The purpose of the program is to prepare students to operate and lead within the global esports and gaming economy while embedding artificial intelligence across decision-making, media production, fan engagement, business operations, and performance systems.
