# AI Compliance Audit

## Purpose
This document provides a program-level framework for reviewing whether courses comply with the SydTek University AI integration and disclosure standards.

## Scope
This audit applies to all core and elective courses in the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

## Review Questions
Each course should be reviewed for the following:

1. Does the course include a documented AI integration file?
2. Does the syllabus describe the role of AI in the course?
3. Does at least one assignment involve AI use, AI critique, or AI-related analysis where appropriate?
4. Are students given clear disclosure expectations?
5. Are ethical considerations addressed?
6. Is evidence of AI-related learning preserved?
7. Are faculty expectations aligned with the AI disclosure and ethics policies?

## Compliance Categories
- Fully Compliant
- Substantially Compliant
- Partially Compliant
- Not Yet Compliant
