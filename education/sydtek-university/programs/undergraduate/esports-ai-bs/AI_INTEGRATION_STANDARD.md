# AI Integration Standard

## Purpose
This document defines the minimum standard for embedding artificial intelligence into the Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance program.

## Minimum Course-Level Requirements
Every course must include:
1. Exposure to at least one relevant AI use case
2. A student activity involving evaluation of AI output
3. Guidance on ethical and responsible use
4. A disclosure process for approved AI-assisted work
5. At least one assignment or reflection connecting AI to the course domain

## Approved AI Use Categories
- Research support
- Drafting and ideation
- Data analysis
- Content planning
- Workflow automation
- Decision support
- Media production assistance
- Reflection and critique

## Restricted or Prohibited Practices
- Undisclosed AI-generated submissions
- Full replacement of student thinking or authorship
- Use of AI in violation of privacy, fairness, or intellectual property expectations
- Fabricated sources or unverifiable claims without disclosure and correction

## Faculty Expectations
Faculty should:
- Design AI-aware assignments
- Clarify approved vs restricted use
- Require critical evaluation of output
- Preserve evidence of AI-related learning
- Update course practices as tools and risks evolve

## Student Expectations
Students must:
- Use approved tools responsibly
- Disclose required AI use
- Verify claims, data, and sources
- Maintain authorship and academic ownership
- Reflect on strengths and weaknesses of AI output
