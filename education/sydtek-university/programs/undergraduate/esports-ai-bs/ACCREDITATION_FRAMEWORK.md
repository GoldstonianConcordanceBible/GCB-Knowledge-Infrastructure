# Accreditation Framework

## Purpose
This document defines the accreditation alignment strategy for the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance at SydTek University.

## Target Accreditation Bodies
This program is structured with alignment toward:
- SACSCOC (Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges)
- HLC (Higher Learning Commission)
- DEAC (Distance Education Accrediting Commission)
