# Admissions and Graduation Standards

## Admissions Expectations
Applicants should possess:
- A high school diploma, recognized equivalent, or approved alternative pathway
- Foundational digital literacy
- Readiness for undergraduate-level reading, writing, communication, and applied technology use

## Recommended Entry Skills
- Basic file management and productivity software familiarity
- Interest in gaming, digital media, business, or technology
- Willingness to engage with AI tools responsibly and reflectively

## Graduation Standards
Students must:
- Complete all required credits in the approved curriculum
- Satisfy institutional GPA and academic standing requirements
- Submit required course artifacts and assessment evidence
- Demonstrate achievement of program learning outcomes
- Meet all academic integrity and AI disclosure expectations
