# Transfer Credit Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how transfer credit may be reviewed and applied to the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

## Policy Statement
SydTek University may award transfer credit for prior academic coursework when such coursework is judged to be comparable in level, scope, rigor, and learning outcomes to courses in the program.

## Eligibility Criteria
Transfer credit may be considered when:
- the coursework was completed at an institution or provider recognized by applicable academic or regulatory standards
- the student earned a grade acceptable under institutional standards
- the course content is substantially equivalent to the SydTek University course being considered
- sufficient documentation is provided for evaluation

## Required Documentation
Students requesting transfer credit should submit:
- official transcript or equivalent academic record
- course description
- syllabus when requested
- additional evidence of course content or learning outcomes when needed

## Evaluation Standards
Transfer credit review may consider:
- course title and description
- credit or contact hours
- level of instruction
- learning outcomes
- assessment methods
- recency of coursework
- alignment to program requirements

## Limits
Transfer credit acceptance may be limited by:
- residency requirements
- capstone or terminal experience requirements
- currency of knowledge in rapidly evolving subject areas such as AI, esports operations, or digital media technologies

## Recording of Decisions
Approved transfer credit decisions should be documented in the student record and retained according to institutional records policy.

## Appeals
Students may request reconsideration by submitting additional documentation for review according to institutional academic procedures.
