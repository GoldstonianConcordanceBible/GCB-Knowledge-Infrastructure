# ESPT 240 – Health, Wellness, and Human Performance in Esports

## Course Code
ESPT 240

## Credits
3

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
None

## Course Description
This course explores the essential dimensions of health and wellness for esports participants. Students examine sleep, ergonomics, stress, nutrition, mental well-being, movement, burnout prevention, and sustainable performance habits in competitive gaming environments. The course also considers how AI and digital tools may assist in health tracking, workload awareness, and performance support while raising important ethical questions.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain key health and wellness challenges facing esports participants.
2. Design strategies to support sustainable performance and well-being.
3. Evaluate the use of digital and AI-supported tools in health and wellness settings.
4. Apply human-centered reasoning to wellness decisions in esports.
