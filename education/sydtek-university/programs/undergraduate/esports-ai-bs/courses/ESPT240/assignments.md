# ESPT 240 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Wellness Risk Reflection
### Purpose
To identify common wellness risks in esports and reflect on their impact.

### Deliverables
- 2-page reflection
- Risk category list

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for brainstorming categories only.

---

## Assignment 2: Esports Performance Plan
### Purpose
To design a sustainable wellness and performance routine for an esports participant.

### Deliverables
- Written plan
- Weekly structure table
- Justification of recommendations

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students must ensure the plan is reasonable, safe, and human-centered.

---

## Assignment 3: AI Wellness Tool Evaluation
### Purpose
To assess the benefits and limitations of an AI-supported or digital wellness tool.

### Deliverables
- 2- to 3-page evaluation
- ethics and risk section

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited evaluative role.
