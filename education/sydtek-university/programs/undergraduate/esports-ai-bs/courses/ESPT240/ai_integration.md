# ESPT 240 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
AI appears in this course primarily as an object of evaluation rather than unquestioned authority. Students study how AI and digital systems may support:
- routine tracking
- fatigue awareness
- schedule reminders
- behavior pattern review

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students evaluate an AI-supported wellness tool or simulated AI-generated routine and assess:
- usefulness
- limitations
- safety concerns
- ethical implications

## Ethical Focus
- privacy
- overmonitoring
- false precision
- misuse of health-related recommendations

## Disclosure
Students must disclose approved AI use in planning and evaluation work.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI Wellness Tool Evaluation is the core AI-learning artifact.
