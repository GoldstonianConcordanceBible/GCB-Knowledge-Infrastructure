# ESPT 110 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Seminar participation
- Short reflective writing
- Ecosystem map artifact
- Final reflection

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Terminology reflection → CLO 1
- Ecosystem map → CLO 1, CLO 2
- Career pathway reflection → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Final seminar reflection → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Content knowledge
- Analysis
- Communication
- Reflection quality
- Ethical AI use where applicable

## Evidence Preservation
The ecosystem map and final reflection should be retained as course artifacts for outcome review.
