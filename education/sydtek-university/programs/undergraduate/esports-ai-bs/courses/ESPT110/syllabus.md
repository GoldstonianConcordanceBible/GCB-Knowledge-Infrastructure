# ESPT 110 – Competitive Gaming Seminar

## Course Code
ESPT 110

## Credits
1

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
None

## Course Description
This introductory seminar explores the esports ecosystem, including its history, major game titles, competition formats, organizational structures, and current industry trends. Through lectures, case studies, guest perspectives, and guided reflection, students gain a broad overview of esports as an academic, cultural, and professional field. The course also introduces students to how artificial intelligence is beginning to shape esports decision-making, media, and industry analysis.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Describe the basic structure and history of the esports industry.
2. Identify major stakeholders, roles, and career pathways in esports.
3. Explain selected current trends influencing competitive gaming.
4. Reflect on the role of AI and digital tools in the future of esports.
