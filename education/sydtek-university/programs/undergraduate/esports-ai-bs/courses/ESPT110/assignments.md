# ESPT 110 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Esports Terminology Reflection
### Purpose
To build foundational vocabulary and conceptual familiarity with esports.

### Deliverables
- Short written reflection
- List of key terms with definitions in student language

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for brainstorming only. Final definitions must be student-authored.

---

## Assignment 2: Esports Ecosystem Map
### Purpose
To identify the main actors and relationships within the esports ecosystem.

### Deliverables
- Visual or written ecosystem map
- 1-page explanation of key relationships

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for structuring ideas. Students must verify all categories and relationships.

---

## Assignment 3: Career Pathway Reflection
### Purpose
To help students connect esports study to possible future roles.

### Deliverables
- 2-page reflection on one or more esports careers
- Skills and preparation plan

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for outlining. Students must disclose use.

---

## Assignment 4: Final Seminar Reflection
### Purpose
To synthesize learning from the course and reflect on AI, trends, and opportunities.

### Deliverables
- 2-page final reflection
- Personal response on the future of esports

### AI Use Guidance
Limited. Students should prioritize personal reflection and original interpretation.
