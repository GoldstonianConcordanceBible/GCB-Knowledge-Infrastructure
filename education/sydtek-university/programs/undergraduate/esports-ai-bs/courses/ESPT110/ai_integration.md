# ESPT 110 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
Students are introduced to AI as a tool for:
- trend scanning
- idea organization
- introductory research support
- reflective critique

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students review an AI-generated summary of an esports trend and identify:
- what appears accurate
- what needs verification
- what may be missing or biased

## Ethical Focus
- AI can oversimplify complex industries
- AI outputs must be verified
- Human judgment remains necessary for interpretation

## Disclosure
Any AI-assisted brainstorming or research support used in graded work must be disclosed.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The final reflection includes a short section evaluating AI's potential role in esports.
