# ESPT 430 – Esports Marketing, Branding, and Audience Growth

## Course Code
ESPT 430

## Credits
3

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
ESPT 220 recommended

## Course Description
This course examines marketing and branding strategies in esports and digital entertainment ecosystems. Students analyze brand identity, audience growth strategies, content marketing, social media ecosystems, partnerships, influencer alignment, and digital campaign execution. The course also explores how artificial intelligence supports audience targeting, content optimization, and engagement forecasting while raising questions around authenticity, algorithmic influence, and brand trust.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain core marketing and branding concepts in esports and digital environments.
2. Analyze audience growth strategies and content ecosystems.
3. Apply AI-supported tools to audience targeting and content optimization.
4. Develop a marketing or branding strategy grounded in audience insight and ethical practice.
