# ESPT 245 – Esports Broadcasting and AI Media Production

## Course Code
ESPT 245

## Credits
3

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
None

## Course Description
In this course, students learn the fundamentals of esports media production, including broadcast writing, active voice, internet streaming, on-location production, anchoring, play-by-play, and digital media workflows. Students also examine economic, ethical, gender, and race issues in esports media. Artificial intelligence is introduced as a support layer for scripting, captioning, tagging, workflow acceleration, and production assistance.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain the core components of esports broadcasting and media production.
2. Produce basic esports media content using structured workflows.
3. Evaluate how AI tools can support production tasks while preserving quality and ethics.
4. Analyze selected social and ethical issues in esports media.

## AI Disclosure Requirement
Required for all assignments involving AI-supported scripting, metadata, or editing assistance.

## Academic Integrity
Students must maintain authorship, disclose AI support, and ensure that creative and factual elements are verified and responsibly presented.

## Instructional Time and Engagement Expectations
Students are expected to complete writing, media observation, production planning, and at least one recorded or simulated media artifact.
