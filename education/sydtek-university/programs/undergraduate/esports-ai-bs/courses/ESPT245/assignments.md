# ESPT 245 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Broadcast Script Assignment
### Purpose
To develop foundational broadcast writing skills for esports media.

### Deliverables
- Short script
- voice and structure notes

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for outline support only. Final script language should be student-developed and edited.

---

## Assignment 2: Production Workflow Plan
### Purpose
To design a practical plan for a basic esports broadcast or media segment.

### Deliverables
- workflow outline
- role assignments
- production checklist

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited way for workflow organization and process critique.

---

## Assignment 3: Final Media Segment
### Purpose
To produce a short esports media artifact demonstrating planning, communication, and technical coordination.

### Deliverables
- recorded or simulated segment
- short reflection
- AI disclosure if applicable

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with full disclosure.
