# ESPT 245 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
AI may support:
- script ideation
- production planning
- caption drafting
- clip tagging
- metadata drafting

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students use AI to help structure a production workflow plan, then evaluate:
- where the AI was useful
- where it lacked production realism
- how human review improved the plan

## Ethical Focus
- authenticity in media creation
- overautomation of creative voice
- bias and representation
- accuracy in metadata and captions

## Disclosure
AI-assisted scripting, workflow planning, or metadata creation must be disclosed.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The production workflow plan is the main AI-learning artifact.
