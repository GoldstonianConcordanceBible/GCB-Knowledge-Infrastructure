# ESPT 420 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion and reflective application
- Written leadership case analysis
- AI-era decision critique
- Applied leadership action plan and presentation

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Leadership theory and case analysis → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI-era decision-making critique → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Personal leadership action plan and presentation → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Leadership understanding
- Analytical reasoning
- Ethical judgment
- Communication and professional presentation
- Human-centered application
- AI critique and disclosure

## Evidence Preservation
The leadership case analysis and AI-era decision critique should be retained as primary assessment artifacts. Leadership action plans may also be retained for program review and leadership development assessment.
