# ESPT 420 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Leadership Theory and Case Analysis
### Purpose
To apply leadership theory to a realistic esports, gaming, or digital organization scenario involving power, conflict, communication, change, or team performance.

### Deliverables
- 4- to 5-page case analysis
- theory-to-case mapping section
- leadership recommendation summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for outlining or theory comparison support only. Students must independently analyze the case and verify all claims.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Era Decision-Making Critique
### Purpose
To evaluate how AI-supported dashboards, recommendations, or data systems influence leadership judgment and organizational behavior.

### Deliverables
- 2- to 3-page critique
- strengths and risks chart
- disclosure record if AI is used in the comparative exercise

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited, comparative way. Students assess differences between their own reasoning and AI-supported framing.

---

## Assignment 3: Personal Leadership Action Plan and Presentation
### Purpose
To develop a practical leadership plan grounded in theory, self-awareness, ethics, and organizational application.

### Deliverables
- written leadership action plan
- presentation or recorded summary
- reflection on leadership growth priorities

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students remain responsible for the final logic, reflection quality, and ethical framing.
