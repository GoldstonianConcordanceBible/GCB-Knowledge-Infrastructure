# ESPT 420 – Leadership in Esports and AI-Era Organizations

## Course Code
ESPT 420

## Credits
4

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
ESPT 210 recommended; BUAD 220 recommended

## Course Description
This course offers a comprehensive review of leadership in esports, gaming, and AI-shaped organizations. Students examine leadership theories, personal leadership development, values and ethics, power, followership, motivation, conflict resolution, multicultural leadership, organizational change, and performance excellence. Special emphasis is placed on leading in environments where AI systems, analytics platforms, digital communities, and rapidly changing technologies influence decision-making, culture, and organizational trust.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain major leadership theories and their relevance to esports and digital organizations.
2. Analyze leadership challenges involving teams, communities, conflict, change, and performance.
3. Evaluate how AI-supported systems and digital environments shape leadership decisions and organizational behavior.
4. Apply ethical, human-centered leadership practices to esports and AI-era organizational scenarios.
