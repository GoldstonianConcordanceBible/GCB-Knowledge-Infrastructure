# ESPT 420 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
AI appears in this course as:
- a decision-support influence on leaders
- a source of recommendations requiring human judgment
- a tool for comparative analysis and reflective critique

Students study how AI may affect:
- staffing and performance decisions
- communication workflows
- organizational dashboards
- bias, fairness, and accountability
- perceptions of authority and objectivity

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students complete an AI-era decision-making critique in which they:
- review an AI-supported recommendation or data-driven leadership scenario
- identify where the system supports useful insight
- identify where it oversimplifies, biases, or distorts leadership judgment
- explain what responsible human oversight looks like

## Ethical Focus
Students should address:
- automation bias
- abdication of leadership responsibility
- unfair or opaque decision logic
- overconfidence in dashboards
- trust and accountability in digital organizations

## Disclosure
Any approved AI-assisted work must be disclosed according to course and program policy.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI-era decision-making critique is the principal AI-learning artifact in this course.
