# ESPT 420 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 420 – Leadership in Esports and AI-Era Organizations

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain major leadership theories and their relevance to esports and digital organizations.
2. Analyze leadership challenges involving teams, communities, conflict, change, and performance.
3. Evaluate how AI-supported systems and digital environments shape leadership decisions and organizational behavior.
4. Apply ethical, human-centered leadership practices to esports and AI-era organizational scenarios.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 7
- CLO 2 → PLO 7, PLO 8
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 7

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Leadership theory and case analysis
- AI-era decision-making critique
- Personal leadership action plan and presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The case analysis and AI critique should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Leadership action plans may be indexed for program review and leadership development analysis.
