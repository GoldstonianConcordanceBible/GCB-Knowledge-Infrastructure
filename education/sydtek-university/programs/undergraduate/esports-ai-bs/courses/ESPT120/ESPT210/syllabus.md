# ESPT 210 – Contemporary Issues in Esports and Emerging Technologies

## Course Code
ESPT 210

## Credits
4

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
ESPT 120 recommended

## Course Description
Students use inquiry, discussion, and research to examine major issues influencing the esports industry. Topics include globalization, labor, economics, race, class, gender, disability, age, governance, platform power, online behavior, and the growing role of emerging technologies including artificial intelligence. The course emphasizes critical thinking, ethical analysis, and evidence-based problem solving.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Identify contemporary issues affecting esports and gaming communities.
2. Analyze the social, cultural, and technological dimensions of esports controversies.
3. Evaluate the impact of AI and emerging technologies on players, organizations, and audiences.
4. Propose thoughtful responses to current problems in esports.
