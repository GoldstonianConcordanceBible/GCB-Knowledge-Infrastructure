# ESPT 120 – Introduction to Esports and AI Systems

## Course Code
ESPT 120

## Credits
4

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
None

## Course Description
This course provides a foundational overview of the esports industry, including financial, legal, ethical, marketing, and operational issues. Students study the structure of esports organizations and examine how artificial intelligence is transforming analytics, media production, fan engagement, talent evaluation, scheduling, and strategic planning across gaming and esports environments.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain the organizational and business foundations of esports.
2. Analyze major legal, ethical, and operational issues in esports.
3. Apply introductory AI tools to esports-related decision-making tasks.
4. Evaluate career and business opportunities in esports and related digital industries.
