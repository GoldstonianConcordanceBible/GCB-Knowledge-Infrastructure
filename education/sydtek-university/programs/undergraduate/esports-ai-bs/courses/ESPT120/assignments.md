# ESPT 120 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Esports Industry Analysis Report
### Purpose
To analyze the structure, business model, and strategic issues of a selected esports organization or segment.

### Deliverables
- 4- to 6-page report
- Brief visual summary

### AI Use Guidance
Allowed for outline generation and comparative analysis support. Students must verify all claims.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Supported Operations Memo
### Purpose
To examine how an esports organization could use AI in scheduling, analytics, content, or operations.

### Deliverables
- Professional memo
- AI usage disclosure appendix

### AI Use Guidance
Required. Students must document prompts, outputs, and revisions.

---

## Assignment 3: Business Opportunity Pitch
### Purpose
To propose a viable esports or gaming-related business or service opportunity.

### Deliverables
- Slide deck
- 5-minute pitch
- Revenue concept summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional but encouraged for ideation and audience analysis.
