# ESPT 120 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 120 – Introduction to Esports and AI Systems

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain the organizational and business foundations of esports.
2. Analyze major legal, ethical, and operational issues in esports.
3. Apply introductory AI tools to esports-related decision-making tasks.
4. Evaluate career and business opportunities in esports and related digital industries.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 1
- CLO 2 → PLO 1, PLO 7
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 6

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Industry analysis report
- AI-supported operations memo
- Business opportunity pitch

## Evidence Collection Notes
The industry analysis report and operations memo should be stored for annual review.
