# ESPT 120 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
AI is used to support:
- research scaffolding
- organizational analysis
- operations planning
- market opportunity exploration
- decision support critique

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students complete an operations memo in which they use AI to propose one operational improvement for an esports organization and then evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of the AI-generated suggestions.

## Ethical Focus
- hallucinated market claims
- biased assumptions about players or audiences
- overreliance on automation in strategic decisions
- need for human oversight

## Disclosure
All AI-supported work must include an AI disclosure appendix.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The operations memo serves as the primary AI-learning artifact.
