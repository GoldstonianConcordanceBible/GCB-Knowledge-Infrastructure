# ESPT 220 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 220 – Social Media Management and AI Content Strategy

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Develop a social media strategy aligned with esports or gaming brand goals.
2. Analyze audience engagement and platform-specific content performance.
3. Use AI tools to support content planning and optimization.
4. Evaluate ethical and strategic issues related to digital audience management.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 3, PLO 6
- CLO 2 → PLO 3, PLO 8
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 3
- CLO 4 → PLO 3, PLO 7

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Brand and audience analysis
- AI-assisted content calendar
- Final strategy presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The content calendar and strategy presentation should be preserved for annual review.
