# ESPT 220 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Brand and Audience Analysis
### Purpose
To identify a brand's goals, audience, platform opportunities, and content challenges.

### Deliverables
- 3- to 4-page analysis
- Audience segment table

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for brainstorming audience categories. Student must justify all conclusions.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Assisted Content Calendar
### Purpose
To create a structured content plan for an esports or gaming brand.

### Deliverables
- 2-week or 4-week content calendar
- AI usage log
- Rationale summary

### AI Use Guidance
Required. Students must show how they edited, filtered, and improved AI output.

---

## Assignment 3: Final Social Strategy Presentation
### Purpose
To present an integrated strategy for growth, engagement, and performance tracking.

### Deliverables
- Slide deck
- Oral presentation
- KPI summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with full disclosure.
