# ESPT 220 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
Students use AI for:
- content idea generation
- scheduling support
- audience segmentation brainstorming
- copy refinement
- campaign reflection

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students build a content calendar with AI assistance and then annotate:
- which outputs were useful
- which were generic or weak
- which required revision for audience fit
- how the final version improved on the AI output

## Ethical Focus
- authenticity vs automation
- overproduction of generic content
- audience manipulation risks
- ownership and disclosure

## Disclosure
The content calendar assignment requires an AI usage log.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI-assisted content calendar serves as the primary AI-learning artifact.
