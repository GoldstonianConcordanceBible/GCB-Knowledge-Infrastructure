# ESPT 220 – Social Media Management and AI Content Strategy

## Course Code
ESPT 220

## Credits
4

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
None

## Course Description
This course equips students with knowledge and practical skills for developing social media strategies in esports and gaming-related environments. Students study audience behavior, platform dynamics, brand identity, engagement planning, and performance measurement. The course also explores how AI supports content ideation, scheduling, copy refinement, sentiment analysis, and campaign optimization.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Develop a social media strategy aligned with esports or gaming brand goals.
2. Analyze audience engagement and platform-specific content performance.
3. Use AI tools to support content planning and optimization.
4. Evaluate ethical and strategic issues related to digital audience management.
