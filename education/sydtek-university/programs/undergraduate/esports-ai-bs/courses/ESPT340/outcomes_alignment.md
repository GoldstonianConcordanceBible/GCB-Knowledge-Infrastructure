# ESPT 340 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 340 – Revenue Generation in Esports and AI-Driven Business Models

## Credits
3

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Identify and explain major revenue streams within esports and digital entertainment.
2. Analyze business models used by esports teams, event operators, creators, and gaming-related ventures.
3. Apply AI-supported tools to market analysis, pricing, forecasting, and business opportunity evaluation.
4. Develop a sustainable revenue or venture strategy for an esports-related organization or initiative.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 1, PLO 6
- CLO 2 → PLO 1, PLO 6
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 6, PLO 7

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Esports revenue model analysis
- AI-supported market and pricing memo
- Final venture or revenue strategy pitch

## Evidence Collection Notes
The revenue model analysis and AI-supported memo should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Final pitch materials may be indexed for future assessment and capstone design review.
