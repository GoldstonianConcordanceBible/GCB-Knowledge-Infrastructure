# ESPT 340 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Esports Revenue Model Analysis
### Purpose
To analyze how an esports organization, event, creator brand, or related venture generates and sustains revenue.

### Deliverables
- 4- to 6-page written analysis
- revenue stream table or visual model
- summary of strengths, weaknesses, and growth opportunities

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for organizing revenue categories and comparative market framing. Students must verify all claims and ensure that conclusions are evidence-based.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Supported Market and Pricing Memo
### Purpose
To use AI tools in a structured and critical way to evaluate market opportunity, audience fit, and pricing logic for an esports-related product, service, or event.

### Deliverables
- professional memo
- pricing or forecasting table
- AI usage log
- short critique of AI output quality

### AI Use Guidance
Required. Students must document how AI was used, what was helpful, what was misleading or weak, and how they revised the analysis.

---

## Assignment 3: Final Venture or Revenue Strategy Pitch
### Purpose
To propose a sustainable revenue strategy or venture concept tied to esports, digital media, gaming, or related performance environments.

### Deliverables
- slide deck
- oral presentation
- 1- to 2-page executive summary
- revenue logic and implementation outline

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with full disclosure. Students remain responsible for the final business logic, evidence, and strategic framing.
