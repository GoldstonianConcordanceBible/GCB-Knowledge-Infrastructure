# ESPT 340 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
Students use AI in this course to support:
- market scanning
- revenue opportunity framing
- pricing scenario generation
- forecast comparison
- sponsorship concept ideation
- business intelligence critique

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students complete an AI-supported market and pricing memo in which they:
- use AI to identify possible opportunity categories or pricing scenarios
- compare AI-generated suggestions against course concepts and human judgment
- identify weak assumptions, generic output, or unverifiable claims
- revise the final recommendation based on evidence and critical review

## Ethical Focus
Students should examine:
- false confidence in AI-generated forecasts
- bias in market assumptions
- shallow or generic business recommendations
- risks of overreliance on automation in entrepreneurship and strategic decisions

## Disclosure
Required AI-supported assignments must include a disclosure record and reflection on revision and ownership.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI-supported market and pricing memo is the principal AI-learning artifact for this course.
