# ESPT 340 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion-based application and critique
- Written business model analysis
- AI-supported pricing and market memo
- Final pitch presentation

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Revenue model analysis → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI-supported market and pricing memo → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Final venture or revenue strategy pitch → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Business and industry knowledge
- Strategic analysis
- Evidence use
- Data interpretation
- Communication and professional presentation
- AI evaluation and disclosure
- Ethical reasoning where applicable

## Evidence Preservation
The revenue model analysis and AI-supported pricing memo should be retained as core assessment artifacts. Final pitch materials may also be retained for program review and capstone pathway development.
