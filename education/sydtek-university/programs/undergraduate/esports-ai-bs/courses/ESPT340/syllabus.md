# ESPT 340 – Revenue Generation in Esports and AI-Driven Business Models

## Course Code
ESPT 340

## Credits
3

## Delivery Format
On-campus, online, hybrid, and MOOC-supported

## Prerequisites
ESPT 120 recommended; BUAD 110 recommended

## Course Description
This course provides students with a deep understanding of monetization, entrepreneurship, and business development in esports and digital entertainment environments. Students examine sponsorships, partnerships, ticketing, subscriptions, advertising, merchandising, licensing, platform economics, creator-driven revenue, and venture opportunities. The course also explores how artificial intelligence supports market analysis, pricing strategy, audience forecasting, revenue modeling, and business intelligence.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Identify and explain major revenue streams within esports and digital entertainment.
2. Analyze business models used by esports teams, event operators, creators, and gaming-related ventures.
3. Apply AI-supported tools to market analysis, pricing, forecasting, and business opportunity evaluation.
4. Develop a sustainable revenue or venture strategy for an esports-related organization or initiative.
