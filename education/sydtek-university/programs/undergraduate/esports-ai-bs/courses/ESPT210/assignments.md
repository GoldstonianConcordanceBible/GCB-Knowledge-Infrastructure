# ESPT 210 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Current Issue Brief
### Purpose
To analyze a major current issue affecting esports using evidence and critical reasoning.

### Deliverables
- 4-page issue brief
- Problem statement
- Stakeholder analysis
- Recommendation section

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for research structuring only. Students must independently verify sources and evidence.

---

## Assignment 2: AI and Emerging Tech Critique
### Purpose
To evaluate the impact of AI or another emerging technology on esports ecosystems.

### Deliverables
- 2- to 3-page critique
- Risk and opportunity matrix

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited way. Students compare their own critique with AI-generated framing and assess differences.

---

## Assignment 3: Policy or Strategy Recommendation Presentation
### Purpose
To propose a response to a contemporary esports issue.

### Deliverables
- Slide deck
- Oral presentation
- Written recommendation summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with full disclosure.
