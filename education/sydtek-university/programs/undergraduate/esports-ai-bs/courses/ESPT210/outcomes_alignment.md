# ESPT 210 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 210 – Contemporary Issues in Esports and Emerging Technologies

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Identify contemporary issues affecting esports and gaming communities.
2. Analyze the social, cultural, and technological dimensions of esports controversies.
3. Evaluate the impact of AI and emerging technologies on players, organizations, and audiences.
4. Propose thoughtful responses to current problems in esports.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 1
- CLO 2 → PLO 1, PLO 7, PLO 8
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 4, PLO 7
- CLO 4 → PLO 7, PLO 8

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Current issue brief
- AI and emerging tech critique
- Recommendation presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The issue brief and AI critique will be stored as primary course evidence.
