# ESPT 210 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion-based analysis
- Written issue brief
- Technology critique
- Final presentation

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Current issue brief → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI and emerging tech critique → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Final recommendation presentation → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Critical analysis
- Evidence use
- Ethical reasoning
- Communication
- AI evaluation and disclosure

## Evidence Preservation
The issue brief and AI critique should be retained for assessment and program review.
