# ESPT 210 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
AI appears in this course in two ways:
1. as a subject of critical study
2. as a limited analytical tool for comparison and critique

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students compare their own analysis of a current esports issue with an AI-generated summary and identify:
- missing context
- oversimplified reasoning
- potential bias
- implications for responsible use

## Ethical Focus
- algorithmic bias
- moderation and surveillance
- inequity and visibility
- labor displacement and platform control

## Disclosure
Any AI-assisted assignment work must be disclosed.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI and Emerging Tech Critique is the principal AI evidence artifact.
