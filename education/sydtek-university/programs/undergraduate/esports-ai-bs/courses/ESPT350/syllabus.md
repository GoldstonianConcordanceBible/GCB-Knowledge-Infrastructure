## Course Code

## Credits

## Prerequisites
ESPT 240 recommended

## Course Description
This course offers an in-depth exploration of the psychological principles that shape performance, decision-making, motivation, resilience, behavior, and team dynamics in esports and related performance environments. Students study confidence, stress, attention, emotional regulation, communication, mindset, burnout, and mental resilience. The course also examines AI-supported behavior modeling, performance dashboards, and digital systems that claim to interpret or optimize player behavior, with attention to ethical and human-centered concerns.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain psychological concepts relevant to performance, motivation, and decision-making.
2. Analyze behavioral, emotional, and cognitive factors affecting performance in esports and related settings.
3. Evaluate how digital and AI-supported tools represent, model, or influence performance-related behavior.
4. Apply psychological and human-centered reasoning to performance improvement strategies.
