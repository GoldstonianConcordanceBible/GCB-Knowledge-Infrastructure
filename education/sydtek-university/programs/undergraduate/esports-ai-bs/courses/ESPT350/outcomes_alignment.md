# ESPT 350 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 350 – Psychology of Performance and Decision-Making

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain psychological concepts relevant to performance, motivation, and decision-making.
2. Analyze behavioral, emotional, and cognitive factors affecting performance in esports and related settings.
3. Evaluate how digital and AI-supported tools represent, model, or influence performance-related behavior.
4. Apply psychological and human-centered reasoning to performance improvement strategies.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 4
- CLO 2 → PLO 4, PLO 7
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 4, PLO 7

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Performance psychology case analysis
- AI behavior modeling critique
- Human-centered performance support plan

## Evidence Collection Notes
The case analysis and AI critique should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Performance support plans may be indexed for ongoing review of cross-course wellness and performance learning.
