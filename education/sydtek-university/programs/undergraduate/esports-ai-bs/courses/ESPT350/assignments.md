# ESPT 350 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Performance Psychology Case Analysis
### Purpose
To analyze a performance scenario using psychological concepts related to motivation, stress, focus, communication, or resilience.

### Deliverables
- 4-page case analysis
- concept-to-case application section
- recommendation summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for brainstorming structure only. Students must independently produce the psychological analysis and verify all claims.

---

## Assignment 2: AI Behavior Modeling Critique
### Purpose
To evaluate how an AI-supported system, dashboard, or behavior model attempts to interpret performance-related behavior.

### Deliverables
- 2- to 3-page critique
- risk and limitation chart
- disclosure record if AI is used in the comparative exercise

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited, comparative way. Students contrast their own reasoning with AI-generated framing and assess differences.

---

## Assignment 3: Human-Centered Performance Support Plan
### Purpose
To design a practical and ethical plan for supporting performance improvement in an esports or related performance setting.

### Deliverables
- written support plan
- intervention rationale
- short presentation or recorded summary

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students remain responsible for safety, logic, and human-centered design.
