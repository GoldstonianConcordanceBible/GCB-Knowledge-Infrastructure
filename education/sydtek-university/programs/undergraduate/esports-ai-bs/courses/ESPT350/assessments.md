# ESPT 350 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion and reflective analysis
- Written case-based evaluation
- AI behavior critique
- Applied performance support plan

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Performance psychology case analysis → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI behavior modeling critique → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Human-centered performance support plan → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Psychological understanding
- Analytical reasoning
- Human-centered judgment
- Ethical evaluation
- Communication
- AI critique and disclosure

## Evidence Preservation
The case analysis and AI behavior modeling critique should be retained as primary assessment artifacts. Performance support plans may also be retained for program review and cross-course assessment of human-centered reasoning.
