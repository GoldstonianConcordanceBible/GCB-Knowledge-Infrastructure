# GAME 145 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion and workshop participation
- Written game analysis
- AI and intelligent systems critique
- Final concept or prototype presentation

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Game analysis reflection → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI and intelligent systems critique → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Basic game concept or prototype presentation → CLO 1, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Design understanding
- Systems thinking
- Analytical reasoning
- Communication and presentation
- Creativity and coherence
- AI critique and disclosure

## Evidence Preservation
The game analysis reflection and AI and intelligent systems critique should be retained as primary assessment artifacts. Final concept materials may also be retained for program review and design pathway development.
