# GAME 145 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Game Analysis Reflection
### Purpose
To analyze how mechanics, rules, feedback, and player experience function in a selected game.

### Deliverables
- 2- to 3-page reflection
- design feature summary
- player experience interpretation

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for organizing observations only. Students must independently analyze the game and explain design logic in their own words.

---

## Assignment 2: AI and Intelligent Systems Critique
### Purpose
To evaluate how AI or intelligent systems function within a game or within a game design workflow.

### Deliverables
- 2-page critique
- strengths and limitations chart
- disclosure record if AI is used in the comparative exercise

### AI Use Guidance
Required in a limited, comparative way. Students may compare their own critique to an AI-generated summary and identify differences.

---

## Assignment 3: Basic Game Concept or Prototype Presentation
### Purpose
To develop and present a simple game concept or early prototype grounded in design principles.

### Deliverables
- concept brief or prototype artifact
- presentation or recorded walkthrough
- short design rationale

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students remain responsible for the final design logic, coherence, and originality of the concept.
