# GAME 145 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
GAME 145 – Introduction to Game Design and Intelligent Systems

## Credits
2

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain foundational principles of game design, systems thinking, and player experience.
2. Analyze how mechanics, rules, and structure shape gameplay and engagement.
3. Evaluate how intelligent systems and AI concepts appear in games and game development workflows.
4. Develop a basic game concept or prototype informed by design principles and iterative feedback.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 1, PLO 5
- CLO 2 → PLO 1, PLO 8
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 5
- CLO 4 → PLO 5

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Game analysis reflection
- AI and intelligent systems critique
- Basic game concept or prototype presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The game analysis and AI critique should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Final concept materials may be indexed for future design-course review and portfolio pathway development.
