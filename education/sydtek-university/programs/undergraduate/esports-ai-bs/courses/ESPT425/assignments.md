# ESPT 425 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Administrative Systems Analysis
### Purpose
To analyze the administrative structure, planning systems, and coordination demands of a sport, esports, or performance-oriented organization.

### Deliverables
- 4- to 5-page analysis
- administrative function map
- summary of strengths, weaknesses, and improvement opportunities

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for organizing categories or outlining structure only. Students must independently interpret the organization and verify all claims.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Supported Operations and Reporting Memo
### Purpose
To use AI tools in a structured and critical way to support an administrative workflow recommendation involving scheduling, reporting, planning, or coordination.

### Deliverables
- professional memo
- workflow or reporting table
- AI usage log
- critique of AI-supported recommendations

### AI Use Guidance
Required. Students must document how AI was used, what it got right, where it was weak or generic, and how they improved the final memo.

---

## Assignment 3: Final Administration Strategy Presentation
### Purpose
To present a coherent administrative strategy for a sport, esports, or performance organization that integrates planning, staffing, reporting, and decision support.

### Deliverables
- slide deck
- oral presentation
- executive summary with implementation notes

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students remain responsible for the final administrative logic, evidence use, and ethical framing.
