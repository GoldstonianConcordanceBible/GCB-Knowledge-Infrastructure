# ESPT 425 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 425 – Sport and Performance Administration in Digital Environments

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain core administrative and organizational functions in sport, esports, and performance settings.
2. Analyze planning, finance, staffing, and coordination challenges in performance-oriented organizations.
3. Apply AI-supported tools to administrative workflows, reporting, and strategic decision support.
4. Develop a coherent administration strategy for a sport, esports, or performance organization.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 1, PLO 7
- CLO 2 → PLO 1, PLO 7, PLO 8
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 7, PLO 8

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Administrative systems analysis
- AI-supported operations and reporting memo
- Final administration strategy presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The systems analysis and AI-supported memo should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Final strategy presentations may be indexed for program review and administrative pathway development.
