# ESPT 425 – Sport and Performance Administration in Digital Environments

## Course Code

## Credits
4

## Prerequisites
BUAD 220 recommended; ESPT 310 recommended

## Course Description
This course provides students with a deep understanding of management and organizational skills required to lead in sports, esports, and performance-oriented organizations. Topics include strategic planning, financial management, scheduling, event coordination, human resource management, athlete and player support, communication, and organizational decision-making. Students also examine how artificial intelligence supports administrative dashboards, forecasting, scheduling, reporting, and operational coordination in digital environments.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Explain core administrative and organizational functions in sport, esports, and performance settings.
2. Analyze planning, finance, staffing, and coordination challenges in performance-oriented organizations.
3. Apply AI-supported tools to administrative workflows, reporting, and strategic decision support.
4. Develop a coherent administration strategy for a sport, esports, or performance organization.
