# ESPT 425 Assessments

## Assessment Structure
- Discussion and applied administrative exercises
- Written systems analysis
- AI-supported operations and reporting memo
- Final administration strategy presentation

## Assessment-to-Outcome Mapping
- Administrative systems analysis → CLO 1, CLO 2
- AI-supported operations and reporting memo → CLO 2, CLO 3
- Final administration strategy presentation → CLO 3, CLO 4

## Rubric Domains Used
- Administrative understanding
- Strategic planning
- Evidence use
- Communication and presentation
- Data interpretation
- AI critique and disclosure
- Ethical reasoning

## Evidence Preservation
The administrative systems analysis and AI-supported operations memo should be retained as primary course assessment artifacts. Final strategy presentations may also be retained for program review and administrative pathway assessment.
