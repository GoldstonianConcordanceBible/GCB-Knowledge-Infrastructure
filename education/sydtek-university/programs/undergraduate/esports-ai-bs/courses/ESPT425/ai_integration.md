# ESPT 425 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
Students use AI in this course to support:
- workflow analysis
- scheduling logic
- reporting structure ideation
- dashboard interpretation support
- planning and coordination reflection

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students complete an AI-supported operations and reporting memo in which they:
- use AI to generate or refine an administrative recommendation
- identify where the AI is useful, shallow, unrealistic, or context-poor
- revise the final workflow or reporting logic using course concepts and organizational judgment
- reflect on efficiency claims versus real administrative responsibility

## Ethical Focus
Students should address:
- overautomation of human coordination
- misuse of dashboards as authority
- weak assumptions in administrative recommendations
- accountability in digitally mediated operations
- risks of efficiency without context

## Disclosure
Required AI-supported assignments must include a disclosure record and explanation of revision and ownership.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI-supported operations and reporting memo is the principal AI-learning artifact in this course.
