## Course Description
This course examines how esports, sports, and digital entertainment organizations engage, grow, and retain audiences in a fragmented media environment. Students study audience behavior, digital touchpoints, community engagement, segmentation, retention, conversion, and the strategic use of data. The course also explores how artificial intelligence supports personalization, audience analysis, campaign optimization, and engagement forecasting, while raising ethical questions around privacy, profiling, and trust.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Analyze fan and audience behavior across digital engagement environments.
2. Design fan engagement strategies that support retention, community growth, and brand value.
3. Use data and AI-supported tools to segment audiences, interpret engagement patterns, and inform personalization strategies.
4. Evaluate ethical issues related to audience data, AI-supported personalization, and digital trust.
