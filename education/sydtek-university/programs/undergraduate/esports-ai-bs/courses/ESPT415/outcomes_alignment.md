# ESPT 415 Outcomes Alignment

## Course
ESPT 415 – Fan Engagement Strategies, Data, and Personalization

## Credits
4

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Analyze fan and audience behavior across digital engagement environments.
2. Design fan engagement strategies that support retention, community growth, and brand value.
3. Use data and AI-supported tools to segment audiences, interpret engagement patterns, and inform personalization strategies.
4. Evaluate ethical issues related to audience data, AI-supported personalization, and digital trust.

## Program Learning Outcome Alignment
- CLO 1 → PLO 3, PLO 8
- CLO 2 → PLO 3, PLO 6
- CLO 3 → PLO 2, PLO 3, PLO 8
- CLO 4 → PLO 3, PLO 7

## Key Assessment Artifacts
- Fan and audience analysis
- AI-supported segmentation and personalization memo
- Integrated fan engagement strategy presentation

## Evidence Collection Notes
The audience analysis and AI-supported memo should be stored as primary outcome evidence. Final strategy presentations may be indexed for advisory review, program assessment, and cross-course marketing alignment.
