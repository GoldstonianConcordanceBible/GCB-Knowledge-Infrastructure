# ESPT 415 AI Integration

## AI Role in the Course
Students use AI in this course to support:
- audience segmentation brainstorming
- engagement pattern interpretation
- personalization concept development
- campaign optimization reflection
- KPI-oriented strategy refinement

## Required AI Learning Activity
Students complete an AI-supported segmentation and personalization memo in which they:
- use AI to generate or refine possible audience segments or personalization ideas
- identify which outputs are useful, generic, or ethically risky
- revise the final strategy using human judgment, course concepts, and contextual evidence
- reflect on trust, profiling, and privacy implications

## Ethical Focus
Students should address:
- overprofiling and stereotyping
- privacy and data-use expectations
- personalization that undermines trust
- opaque recommendation logic
- bias in engagement assumptions

## Disclosure
Required AI-supported assignments must include a disclosure record and explanation of revision and ownership.

## Evidence of AI Learning
The AI-supported segmentation and personalization memo is the principal AI-learning artifact in this course.
