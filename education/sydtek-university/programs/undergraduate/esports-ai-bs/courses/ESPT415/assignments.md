# ESPT 415 Assignments

## Assignment 1: Fan and Audience Analysis
### Purpose
To analyze audience behavior, engagement patterns, and community characteristics for an esports, gaming, or digital entertainment brand.

### Deliverables
- 4-page analysis
- audience segment summary
- KPI interpretation section

### AI Use Guidance
Optional for organizing segment ideas only. Students must independently interpret the audience context and verify all claims.

---

## Assignment 2: AI-Supported Segmentation and Personalization Memo
### Purpose
To use AI tools in a structured and critical way to support segmentation logic and personalization strategy design.

### Deliverables
- professional memo
- segment-to-strategy table
- AI usage log
- ethical reflection section

### AI Use Guidance
Required. Students must document how the AI was used, identify generic or risky outputs, and show how the final memo improved on the AI-generated material.

---

## Assignment 3: Integrated Fan Engagement Strategy Presentation
### Purpose
To present a cross-channel strategy for fan engagement, retention, and audience value creation.

### Deliverables
- slide deck
- oral presentation
- strategy summary with KPIs and implementation notes

### AI Use Guidance
Optional with disclosure. Students remain responsible for the final logic, evidence, and ethics of the strategy.
