# Course Hours Tracking

## Purpose
To document expected student engagement hours connected to course media, playlists, labs, readings, discussions, and applied assignments.

## Categories to Track
- Direct instruction
- Playlist or video engagement
- Reading time
- Discussion time
- Applied work
- Project development
- Assessment preparation

## Use
Faculty may use this file and related playlist mappings to demonstrate instructional activity and support internal review, audit readiness, and future accreditation processes.
