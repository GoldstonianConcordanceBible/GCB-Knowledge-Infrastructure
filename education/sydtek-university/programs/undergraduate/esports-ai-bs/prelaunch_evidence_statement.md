# Pre-Launch Evidence Statement

## Purpose
This document clarifies how evidence should be interpreted during the pre-launch development phase of the Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance.

## Current Status
The program is in a structured pre-launch development stage. Repository contents currently include:
- governance documents
- curriculum architecture
- assessment plans
- course development files
- data templates
- AI integration standards
- evidence collection templates

## Evidence Available Now
At this stage, the repository provides evidence of:
- academic planning
- governance readiness
- outcomes mapping
- assessment design
- documentation systems
- compliance intent
- audit remediation planning

## Evidence Not Yet Fully Available
Because the program is pre-launch, the repository may not yet contain:
- live student data
- completed faculty assignment records
- active term assessment results
- completed capstone artifacts
- advisory meeting minutes from active operations
- full historical course revision trails

## Reviewer Guidance
Pre-launch reviewers should assess this repository primarily for:
- structural completeness
- coherence of program design
- policy readiness
- evidence architecture
- compliance preparation
- launch readiness

## Transition to Active Evidence
As the program launches, the following evidence categories should be populated in sequence:
1. faculty assignments and qualifications
2. course delivery records
3. student artifacts and assessment summaries
4. KPI and outcomes data
5. annual review and closing-the-loop records
