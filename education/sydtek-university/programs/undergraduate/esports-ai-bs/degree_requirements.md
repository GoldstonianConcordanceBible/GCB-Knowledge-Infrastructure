# Degree Requirements

The Bachelor of Science in Esports, Gaming, and AI Performance requires a total of 49 semester hours.

## Core Coursework: 40 Semester Hours
- ESPT 110 – Competitive Gaming Seminar (1)
- ESPT 120 – Introduction to Esports and AI Systems (4)
- ESPT 210 – Contemporary Issues in Esports and Emerging Technologies (4)
- ESPT 220 – Social Media Management and AI Content Strategy (4)
- ESPT 240 – Health, Wellness, and Human Performance in Esports (3)
- ESPT 245 – Esports Broadcasting and AI Media Production (3)
- ESPT 310 – Live Event Management and Intelligent Operations (4)
- ESPT 325 – Esport Coaching, Analytics, and Performance Optimization (3)
- ESPT 330 – Live Event Production and Streaming Technologies (3)
- ESPT 340 – Revenue Generation in Esports and AI-Driven Business Models (3)
- ESPT 350 – Psychology of Performance and Decision-Making (4)
- ESPT 415 – Fan Engagement Strategies, Data, and Personalization (4)
- ESPT 420 – Leadership in Esports and AI-Era Organizations (4)
- ESPT 425 – Sport and Performance Administration in Digital Environments (4)
- GAME 145 – Introduction to Game Design and Intelligent Systems (2)
- BUAD 110 – Introduction to Business, Entrepreneurship, and AI Decision Tools (3)
- BUAD 220 – Principles of Business Management in Intelligent Enterprises (3)

## Electives: 9 Semester Hours
Students complete 9 semester hours from approved electives in esports, gaming, business, data, or AI-related study.

## Graduation Expectations
To graduate, students must:
- Complete all required credits
- Meet minimum institutional academic standards
- Demonstrate achievement of program learning outcomes
- Complete required assessments and portfolio artifacts
- Satisfy any capstone or final integrative requirements adopted by SydTek University
