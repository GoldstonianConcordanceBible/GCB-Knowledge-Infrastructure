# Admissions Profile
## Furniture Manufacturing Program

---

## Intended Student Profile

This program is designed for students who are interested in:

- woodworking
- furniture design
- skilled trades
- hands-on building
- manufacturing entrepreneurship
- physical product businesses

---

## Entry Requirements

Recommended entry readiness includes:

- willingness to work with tools
- ability to follow safety procedures
- interest in workshop learning
- commitment to documentation and completion

No prior professional woodworking experience is required for entry-level participation.

---

## Ideal Outcomes for Graduates

Graduates of this program may pursue:

- employment in furniture shops
- independent woodworking businesses
- custom table and chair production
- restoration and finishing work
- trade instruction and apprenticeship pathways
