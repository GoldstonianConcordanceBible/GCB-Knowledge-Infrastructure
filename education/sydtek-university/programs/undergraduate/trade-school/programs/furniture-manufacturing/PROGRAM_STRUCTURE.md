# Program Structure
## Furniture Manufacturing

Duration: 12 Months  
Credential: Certificate in Furniture Manufacturing and Wood Product Fabrication

---

## Term 1 — Foundations of Woodcraft

Students begin with safety, tools, measurements, lumber science, and workshop discipline.

Core focus areas:

- shop safety
- tool identification
- measuring and layout
- wood species and grain
- introduction to hand tools
- introduction to power tools

---

## Term 2 — Structural Furniture Construction

Students move into applied building and structural understanding.

Core focus areas:

- joinery systems
- frame assembly
- tabletop construction
- chair structure
- rocking chair geometry
- load-bearing design

---

## Term 3 — Production and Finishing Systems

Students learn to produce furniture consistently and professionally.

Core focus areas:

- workflow planning
- milling systems
- sanding systems
- staining and sealing
- defect correction
- production repeatability

---

## Term 4 — Commercial Capstone

Students complete commercial-ready builds and prepare products for market.

Core focus areas:

- capstone fabrication
- quality control
- pricing logic
- product documentation
- photography and listings
- customer-facing presentation

---

## Instructional Method

The program uses a build-centered instructional model.

Students learn through:

- demonstrations
- guided builds
- repeat practice
- lab documentation
- production critique
- portfolio assessment
