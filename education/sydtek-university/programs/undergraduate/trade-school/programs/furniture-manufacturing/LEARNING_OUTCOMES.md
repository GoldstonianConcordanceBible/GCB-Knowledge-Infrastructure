# Learning Outcomes
## Furniture Manufacturing Program

Students who complete this program will demonstrate competency in the following areas.

---

## Technical Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- identify major wood species used in furniture construction
- distinguish hardwood and softwood applications
- measure and mark materials accurately
- cut and mill lumber safely
- assemble structural furniture frames
- execute basic and intermediate joinery
- prepare surfaces for finishing
- apply appropriate finishing materials

---

## Manufacturing Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- interpret build sequences
- organize a production workflow
- maintain clean and efficient workstations
- identify quality defects
- perform corrective fabrication steps
- prepare furniture pieces for commercial use

---

## Documentation Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- document a build process step by step
- create materials lists and cut lists
- publish workshop notes in GitHub
- organize project folders clearly
- produce portfolio evidence for each build

---

## Commercial Outcomes

Students will be able to:

- describe a furniture product professionally
- prepare a product for online display
- contribute to a small manufacturing brand
- understand how workshop production connects to commerce
