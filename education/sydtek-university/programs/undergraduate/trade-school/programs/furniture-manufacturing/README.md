# Furniture Manufacturing
## SydTek University Trade School

Program Type: Skilled Trade Certificate  
Division: Skilled Trades and Applied Manufacturing  
Primary Training Lab: Big Boy Furniture  

---

## Program Overview

The Furniture Manufacturing program at SydTek University prepares students to design, build, finish, document, and commercialize handcrafted furniture products.

This program combines:

- traditional woodworking
- manufacturing workflow design
- tool certification
- product engineering
- digital documentation
- ecommerce readiness

Students learn by building real furniture in a workshop environment and documenting each stage of production through GitHub.

---

## Program Purpose

The purpose of this program is to develop students into skilled furniture builders who can:

- understand wood as a structural and aesthetic material
- operate tools safely and efficiently
- fabricate tables, chairs, rocking chairs, and other furniture
- document manufacturing systems
- contribute to real product lines
- launch their own furniture brands or work in production shops

---

## Use Case Lab

The primary example lab for this program is Big Boy Furniture.

Big Boy Furniture is used as a real-world training environment where students study:

- large table construction
- chair systems
- rocking chair geometry
- heirloom furniture finishing
- commercial woodworking workflows

Big Boy Furniture is a use case and lab environment within the SydTek University Trade School system.

---

## Credential

Certificate in Furniture Manufacturing and Wood Product Fabrication

---

## Program Outcomes

By the end of the program, students will be able to:

1. identify and select lumber for furniture builds
2. apply safe workshop practices
3. use hand tools and power tools correctly
4. execute standard woodworking joinery
5. fabricate commercial furniture pieces
6. apply finishing systems
7. document projects in GitHub
8. prepare furniture products for digital sale

---

## Program Phases

The program is divided into four instructional phases:

1. Foundations of Woodcraft
2. Structural Furniture Construction
3. Production and Finishing Systems
4. Commercial Capstone

---

## Capstone Requirement

Each student must complete one finished furniture product and publish:

- build documentation
- materials list
- cut list
- process photography
- final product presentation
- product sales description
