# SydTek University Trade School

Institution: SydTek University  
Division: Skilled Trades and Applied Manufacturing

---

## Overview

The SydTek University Trade School trains students in skilled trades using a modern open curriculum delivered through GitHub, hands-on laboratories, and real-world production environments.

Unlike traditional trade schools, SydTek integrates:

• workshop training  
• real manufacturing projects  
• digital documentation  
• Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)  
• AI-assisted learning systems

Students do not only learn theory — they build real products and businesses.

---

## Educational Philosophy

SydTek Trade School operates under three principles:

1. Build real things
2. Document everything
3. Teach the next generation

Students graduate with both:

• trade skills  
• documented proof of work

## Example Use Case

One example training lab is:

Big Boy Furniture

Students learn furniture manufacturing by building:

• hardwood tables  
• chairs  
• rocking chairs  
• farmhouse furniture

These products are sold through ecommerce channels.

Big Boy Furniture is a **training environment**, not the entire trade program.
