# Course Map
## Furniture Manufacturing Curriculum

---

## Term 1 — Foundations of Woodcraft

- FMT 101 — Workshop Safety and Shop Discipline
- FMT 102 — Lumber Science and Material Selection
- FMT 103 — Measuring, Layout, and Dimensional Accuracy
- FMT 104 — Hand Tools and Manual Woodcraft
- FMT 105 — Power Tools and Machine Operation

---

## Term 2 — Structural Furniture Construction

- FMT 201 — Joinery Systems for Furniture
- FMT 202 — Table Construction Studio
- FMT 203 — Chair Design and Structural Balance
- FMT 204 — Rocking Chair Geometry and Motion
- FMT 205 — Furniture Assembly and Structural Correction

---

## Term 3 — Production and Finishing Systems

- FMT 301 — Workflow Design for Small-Scale Manufacturing
- FMT 302 — Milling, Sanding, and Surface Preparation
- FMT 303 — Furniture Finishing Systems
- FMT 304 — Defect Detection and Repair
- FMT 305 — Documentation, Product Narratives, and Commerce Preparation

---

## Term 4 — Commercial Capstone

- FMT 401 — Capstone Planning and Product Selection
- FMT 402 — Capstone Fabrication
- FMT 403 — Quality Control and Commercial Readiness
- FMT 404 — Product Presentation and Photography
- FMT 405 — Final Portfolio and Sales Listing
