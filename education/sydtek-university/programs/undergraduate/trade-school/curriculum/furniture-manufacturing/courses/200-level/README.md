# 200-Level Courses
## Structural Furniture Construction
## SydTek University Trade School

The 200-level sequence transitions students from tool practice into structural furniture fabrication.

Students move from isolated exercises into real furniture systems such as tables, chairs, and structural frames.

The emphasis shifts toward:

- structural integrity
- joinery precision
- load-bearing design
- furniture balance
- real assembly workflows

---

## 200-Level Course List

FMT 201 — Joinery Systems for Furniture  
FMT 202 — Table Construction Studio  
FMT 203 — Chair Design and Structural Balance  
FMT 204 — Rocking Chair Geometry and Motion  
FMT 205 — Furniture Assembly and Structural Correction

---

## Purpose of the 200-Level Sequence

This stage transforms students from tool users into furniture builders.

Students learn how furniture structures function and how joints, angles, and support systems create durable products.
