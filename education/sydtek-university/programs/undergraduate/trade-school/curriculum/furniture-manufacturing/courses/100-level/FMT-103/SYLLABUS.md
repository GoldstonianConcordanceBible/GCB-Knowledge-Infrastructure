# Syllabus
## FMT 103 — Measuring, Layout, and Dimensional Accuracy

Duration: 4 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

### Week 1 — Measuring Systems
- reading standard measurements
- fractions and increments
- measurement transfer

### Week 2 — Layout Tools and Marking
- pencils
- marking knives
- combination squares
- straightedges
- marking gauges

### Week 3 — Reference Systems and Squareness
- reference face
- reference edge
- checking square
- avoiding drift in repeated cuts

### Week 4 — Dimensional Control in Build Preparation
- preparing cut lists
- laying out stock
- checking dimensions before cutting
- correcting minor layout errors

---

## Completion Standard

Students must show that they can measure, mark, and verify material accurately enough for beginner furniture fabrication.
