# Syllabus
## FMT 104 — Hand Tools and Manual Woodcraft

Duration: 4 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

### Week 1 — Hand Tool Familiarization
- identifying bench tools
- safe carrying and storage
- when to choose hand tools over machines
- workbench habits

### Week 2 — Hand Saws and Cutting Control
- saw types and purposes
- starting a cut
- following a line
- reducing tear-out
- maintaining control through the cut

### Week 3 — Chisels, Mallets, and Hand Planes
- chisel safety
- paring and cleanup cuts
- mallet-assisted work
- basic hand planing
- flattening and edge refinement

### Week 4 — Fitting, Adjustment, and Manual Finish Work
- correcting imperfect cuts
- refining joints
- smoothing edges
- work-holding and clamping
- instructor evaluation and sign-off

---

## Completion Standard

Students must demonstrate basic confidence and control when using core hand tools for beginner furniture tasks.
