# Syllabus
## FMT 102 — Lumber Science and Material Selection

Duration: 4 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

### Week 1 — Introduction to Wood as Material
- living structure of wood
- furniture applications of lumber
- hardwood versus softwood

### Week 2 — Grain, Stability, and Movement
- grain direction
- expansion and contraction
- seasonal movement
- warping, cupping, and twisting

### Week 3 — Defects and Board Quality
- knots
- checks
- splits
- bowing
- cupping
- twist

### Week 4 — Selection Logic for Furniture Projects
- matching material to build type
- selecting boards for tables
- selecting boards for chairs
- selecting boards for visible surfaces

---

## Completion Standard

Students must demonstrate basic confidence in selecting appropriate lumber for a furniture project.
