# Syllabus
## FMT 201 — Joinery Systems for Furniture

Duration: 5 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

Week 1 — Introduction to Structural Joinery  
• why joints fail  
• joint selection logic  
• demonstration of joinery samples  

Week 2 — Basic Furniture Joints  
• butt joints  
• lap joints  
• reinforcement strategies  

Week 3 — Mortise and Tenon Systems  
• layout methods  
• cutting techniques  
• fitting adjustments  

Week 4 — Dowels and Mechanical Joinery  
• dowel alignment  
• jig systems  
• pocket screw systems  

Week 5 — Joinery Evaluation  
• correcting poor joints  
• joint stress testing  
• instructor evaluation
