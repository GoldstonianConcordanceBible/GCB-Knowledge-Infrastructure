# Term 2 Overview
## Structural Furniture Construction

Term 2 moves students into full furniture fabrication.

Students begin constructing real furniture pieces and learn how structural decisions affect durability, balance, and long-term stability.

---

## Courses in Term 2

FMT 201 — Joinery Systems for Furniture  
FMT 202 — Table Construction Studio  
FMT 203 — Chair Design and Structural Balance  
FMT 204 — Rocking Chair Geometry and Motion  
FMT 205 — Furniture Assembly and Structural Correction

---

## Term 2 Outcomes

Students completing Term 2 should demonstrate:

- reliable joinery execution
- structural furniture assembly
- stable furniture construction
- dimensional accuracy in builds
- early furniture design understanding

---

## Instructional Philosophy

Students now move from practice boards to full objects.

This stage emphasizes building complete furniture systems rather than isolated components.
