# 100-Level Courses
## Furniture Manufacturing
## SydTek University Trade School

The 100-level sequence introduces students to the foundational knowledge, habits, and technical competencies required for success in furniture manufacturing.

These courses establish the discipline of the workshop before students move into structural fabrication and commercial production.

---

## 100-Level Course List

- FMT 101 — Workshop Safety and Shop Discipline
- FMT 102 — Lumber Science and Material Selection
- FMT 103 — Measuring, Layout, and Dimensional Accuracy
- FMT 104 — Hand Tools and Manual Woodcraft
- FMT 105 — Power Tools and Machine Operation

---

## Purpose of the 100-Level Sequence

The purpose of this sequence is to ensure that students can:

- work safely
- identify and handle materials correctly
- measure with accuracy
- use foundational tools properly
- develop repeatable workshop habits

These courses are required before students advance into furniture construction labs.
