# FMT 104
## Hand Tools and Manual Woodcraft

Course Code: FMT 104  
Course Level: 100  
Term Placement: Term 1 — Foundations of Woodcraft

---

## Course Description

FMT 104 introduces students to the core hand tools used in furniture making and trains them in controlled manual woodworking techniques.

Students learn how to use hand saws, chisels, mallets, hand planes, layout tools, rasps, files, clamps, and other essential bench tools. The course emphasizes body control, tool respect, material sensitivity, and craftsmanship through repetition.

This course develops the touch and discipline that power-assisted fabrication alone cannot provide.

---

## Course Purpose

The purpose of this course is to develop foundational craftsmanship through manual control.

Students learn to shape, trim, adjust, fit, and refine wood by hand so they can better understand materials, tolerances, and joinery before depending fully on machines.

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

- identify the primary hand tools used in furniture work
- demonstrate safe handling of bench tools
- select the correct hand tool for a specific task
- cut simple lines with a hand saw
- pare and clean wood with chisels
- flatten or refine stock with a hand plane
- clamp and stabilize work correctly
- perform basic fitting and refinement by hand

---

## Lab Requirements

Students are required to participate in:

- hand tool identification drills
- grip and body-position training
- hand saw cutting practice
- chisel control exercises
- planing and flattening demonstrations
- edge-cleaning and fitting work
- clamping and work-holding drills

---

## Assessment Criteria

Students are assessed on:

- tool identification accuracy
- safety in tool handling
- body control and work posture
- cutting consistency
- edge cleanliness
- ability to refine work manually
- care of hand tools during and after use

---

## Required Deliverables

- hand tool identification worksheet
- saw line practice board
- chisel control exercise
- planing and surface correction exercise
- hand-fitting reflection
