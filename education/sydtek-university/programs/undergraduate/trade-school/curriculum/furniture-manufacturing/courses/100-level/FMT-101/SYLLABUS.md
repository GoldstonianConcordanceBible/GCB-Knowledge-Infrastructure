# Syllabus
## FMT 101 — Workshop Safety and Shop Discipline

Duration: 4 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

### Week 1 — Workshop Orientation
- introduction to the shop
- instructor expectations
- walkthrough of work zones
- emergency procedures

### Week 2 — PPE and Hazard Awareness
- eye protection
- hearing protection
- respiratory protection
- clothing and footwear standards
- hazard recognition practice

### Week 3 — Shop Conduct and Shared Space Discipline
- movement around machines
- carrying materials safely
- cleanup systems
- maintaining clear surfaces and pathways

### Week 4 — Safety Demonstration and Evaluation
- safety review
- lab readiness observation
- documentation completion
- instructor sign-off

---

## Completion Standard

Students must demonstrate safe behavior consistently before advancing into applied tool work.
