# Syllabus
## FMT 105 — Power Tools and Machine Operation

Duration: 4 Weeks

---

## Weekly Sequence

### Week 1 — Introduction to Power Tools
- drills and drivers
- orbital sanders
- routers
- portable saw systems
- power tool safety review

### Week 2 — Stationary Machines and Shop Procedures
- miter saw
- table saw
- bandsaw
- planer
- jointer
- machine zones and safe movement

### Week 3 — Setup, Operation, and Material Control
- pre-operation checks
- fence and guide awareness
- feed direction
- body positioning
- material support and control

### Week 4 — Supervised Practice and Readiness Evaluation
- guided operations
- correction of unsafe habits
- cleanup and reset procedures
- instructor sign-off for progression

---

## Completion Standard

Students must demonstrate safe readiness for supervised machine use before advancing into structural fabrication courses.
