AIS-320 Course Shell
AI Infrastructure and MLOps

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

Catalog Description

Introduces the infrastructure required to support artificial intelligence systems in development and production including experiment tracking, model versioning, deployment pipelines, inference services, monitoring, and reproducibility practices.

Students learn how to connect model development to durable operational workflows.

Prerequisites

AIS-210  
AIS-220

Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1 explain the purpose of infrastructure and MLOps in AI system lifecycles
2 design reproducible workflows for model development and deployment
3 evaluate versioning, experiment tracking, and monitoring strategies
4 analyze operational risks in model deployment and maintenance
5 document infrastructure design and workflow decisions through inspectable artifacts

Open Discussion Pedagogy Note

Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment

Mirror: represent infrastructure components, workflows, and deployment logic accurately  
Water: interpret system performance, reproducibility, and operational tradeoffs  
Fire: build and document AI infrastructure and MLOps pipelines
