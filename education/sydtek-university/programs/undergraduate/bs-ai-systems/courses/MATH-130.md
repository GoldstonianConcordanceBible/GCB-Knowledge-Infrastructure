MATH-130 Course Shell
Linear Algebra for AI

Credits: 3  
Level: Undergraduate

Catalog Description

Introduces the linear algebra concepts most relevant to artificial intelligence including vectors, matrices, matrix operations, linear transformations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and tensor-oriented thinking.

Students develop the mathematical foundation needed to understand model representation, optimization, and neural computation.

Prerequisites

None

Course Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

1 explain vectors, matrices, and linear transformations
2 perform core operations used in AI and machine learning contexts
3 analyze how linear algebra supports model computation
4 apply mathematical reasoning to simple AI-related examples
5 document mathematical work and interpretation clearly
