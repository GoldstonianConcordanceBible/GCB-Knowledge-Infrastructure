# BS-AIS Program Outcome Map
Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence Systems

Institution: SydTek University

## Purpose

This document maps BS-AIS program learning outcomes to key courses in the curriculum in order to support curriculum review, program coherence, and accreditation-oriented assessment.

## Program Learning Outcomes

PLO-1  
Explain foundational concepts in artificial intelligence, machine learning, neural networks, and intelligent systems.

PLO-2  
Apply mathematics, statistics, and computational reasoning to AI problems.

PLO-3  
Build, evaluate, and refine machine learning and deep learning models.

PLO-4  
Design reproducible AI workflows using documented datasets, code, and evaluation methods.

PLO-5  
Analyze ethical, social, and governance issues related to AI systems.

PLO-6  
Communicate AI findings through technical writing, repository documentation, and inspectable artifacts.

PLO-7  
Design AI systems that integrate models, infrastructure, and deployment considerations.

PLO-8  
Complete a substantial capstone project demonstrating undergraduate-level mastery in AI systems.

## Outcome Map Key

I = Introduced  
R = Reinforced  
M = Mastered

## Accreditation Utility

This outcome map helps demonstrate:

- curricular coherence
- staged learning progression
- alignment between courses and program outcomes
- capstone culmination of program-level mastery
- identifiable points for direct and indirect assessment

## Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment

Mirror is visible where students accurately represent concepts, data, models, systems, and evidence.  
Water is visible where students interpret patterns, tradeoffs, implications, and ethical tensions.  
Fire is visible where students refine, document, integrate, and present substantial AI work through inspectable academic artifacts.
