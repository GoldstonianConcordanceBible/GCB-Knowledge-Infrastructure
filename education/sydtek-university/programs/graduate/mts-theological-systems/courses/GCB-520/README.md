# GCB-520 — Advanced Eschatology

Institution: SydTek University  
Program: Master of Theological Systems (MTS)  
Credits: 3  
Level: Graduate  

## Course Description
This course examines the theology of last things through Revelation, prophetic literature, apocalyptic symbolism, and competing eschatological models. Students compare interpretive traditions and build structured timelines, symbol systems, and theological frameworks.

## Learning Outcomes
Students will be able to:
- analyze apocalyptic texts at graduate level
- compare premillennial, amillennial, and postmillennial views
- interpret major eschatological symbols
- produce a structured end-times model
- connect prophecy to doctrine responsibly

## Major Outputs
- eschatology comparison paper
- apocalyptic symbol map
- final timeline model

## Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment
Mirror: represent prophetic texts faithfully  
Water: compare schools of interpretation  
Fire: synthesize a usable eschatological system
