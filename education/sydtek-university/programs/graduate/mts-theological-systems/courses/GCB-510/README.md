# GCB-510 — Biblical Mysteries and Hidden Knowledge Systems

Institution: SydTek University  
Program: Master of Theological Systems (MTS)  
Credits: 3  
Level: Graduate  

## Course Description
This course studies difficult and contested biblical themes, including Genesis 6, Nephilim traditions, fallen angels, long lifespans, and hidden knowledge frameworks. Students examine canonical and extra-canonical materials while evaluating symbolic, literal, and hybrid interpretations.

## Learning Outcomes
Students will be able to:
- analyze contested mystery passages in scripture
- compare canonical and extra-canonical materials
- distinguish interpretive models
- formalize theological mystery frameworks
- produce research grounded in sources

## Major Outputs
- mystery theme research paper
- ontology map of entities and concepts
- interpretation matrix

## Mirror → Water → Fire Alignment
Mirror: preserve source integrity  
Water: evaluate competing interpretations  
Fire: build a coherent mystery system
