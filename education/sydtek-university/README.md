# SydTek University

SydTek University represents the academic layer of the Goldstonian Concordance Bible ecosystem.

The university exists to translate knowledge into structured educational programs.

---

## Academic Purpose

SydTek University develops academic programs in:

• theology  
• philosophy  
• decentralized systems  
• artificial intelligence  
• knowledge infrastructure  

Programs may use machine-readable books as foundational texts.

---

## Relationship to GCB

SydTek University operates as an educational institution within the broader Goldstonian Concordance Bible ecosystem.

GCB provides knowledge architecture.

SydTek University provides academic delivery.

---

## Educational Philosophy

Education should be:

• rigorous  
• interdisciplinary  
• technically informed  
• philosophically grounded  

Students learn not only to consume knowledge but also to construct knowledge systems.
