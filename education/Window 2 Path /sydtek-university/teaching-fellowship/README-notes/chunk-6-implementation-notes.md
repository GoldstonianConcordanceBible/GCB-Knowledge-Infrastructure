# Chunk 6 Implementation Notes

This file adds the policy and risk-control layer for the Teaching Fellowship.

Previous chunks established:
- institutional structure,

- and supervision.

This chunk establishes:
- child safeguarding expectations,
- adult boundary rules,
- reporting responsibilities,
- privacy controls,
- and ethical conduct requirements.

## Why this matters for accreditation

A practicum model involving minors is not institutionally credible unless the institution can show:
- clear conduct standards,
- structured supervision,
- risk management,
- reporting pathways,
- and privacy protection.

This file helps demonstrate that SydTek University is not treating K–12 practicum activity informally. It shows that the institution understands that child safety, confidentiality, and ethical boundaries are part of academic quality and institutional governance.

## Operational note

Before implementation, SydTek University should have qualified legal counsel review this policy against:
- state child abuse reporting laws,
- background screening rules,
- homeschool and private-school operational requirements,
- privacy obligations,
- and any state-specific rules affecting minors in online learning environments.

## Privacy note

If instructional recordings or student work are used as fellowship evidence, the institution should pair this file with a dedicated privacy and records policy that addresses:
- consent,
- access control,
- redaction,
- retention,
- and public sharing restrictions.
