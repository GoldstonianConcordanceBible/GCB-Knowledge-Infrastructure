### Notes

This release establishes the foundational machine-readable theological infrastructure for the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem.

It includes doctrine structure, AI routing, canon datasets, terminology controls, and linked-data ontology files for future knowledge graph development, theological retrieval, and SydTek-aligned curriculum applications.
