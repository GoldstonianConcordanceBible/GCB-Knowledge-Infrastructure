# B Corp Governance Layer

This directory defines the governance, impact, accountability, and stakeholder framework for the B Corp candidacy pathway associated with the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem and its affiliated educational, publishing, and knowledge-infrastructure initiatives.

## Purpose

This governance layer exists to:

- establish a credible institutional foundation for B Corp candidacy
- document stakeholder-oriented governance principles
- demonstrate public benefit intent through education, publishing, ethical AI, and community impact
- provide machine-readable artifacts for AI agents, evaluators, auditors, and future institutional partners
- create a GitHub-native evidence trail that supports transparency, continuity, and long-term trust

## Strategic Role

This folder serves four functions simultaneously:

1. **Certification Layer**  
   It structures the organization’s B Corp candidacy narrative and supporting materials in a format aligned with impact-oriented institutional review.

2. **Governance Layer**  
   It defines how decision-making, accountability, ethics, and stakeholder commitments are documented and maintained.

3. **Evidence Layer**  
   It stores policies, standards, and operating frameworks that demonstrate real practices rather than aspirational branding alone.

4. **Machine-Readable Layer**  
   It converts governance and impact into structured JSON artifacts so that AI agents, search systems, and external evaluators can interpret the organization consistently.

## Core Files

- `B_CORP_CANDIDACY_STATEMENT.md`  
  Foundational statement of intent, mission alignment, and candidacy rationale.

- `IMPACT_ASSESSMENT.md`  
  Working assessment of the organization’s impact posture across governance, workers, community, environment, and customers.

- `GOVERNANCE_MODEL.md`  
  Governance logic, authority layers, and decision principles.

- `STAKEHOLDER_COMMITMENTS.md`  
  Public commitments to stakeholders, including learners, families, contributors, readers, partners, and communities.

- `TRANSPARENCY_AND_ACCOUNTABILITY.md`  
  Disclosure, verification, and public accountability framework.

- `LEGAL_STRUCTURE.md`  
  Description of the legal and organizational structure supporting the mission.

- `METRICS_AND_KPIS.md`  
  Defined metrics for tracking impact and operational maturity.

- `ROADMAP_TO_CERTIFICATION.md`  
  Stepwise roadmap for moving from candidacy posture to formal certification readiness.

## Subdirectories

### `EVIDENCE/`
Operational proof layer containing policy, governance, operational, and impact documentation.

### `SCHEMAS/`
JSON schemas used to validate machine-readable governance and impact artifacts.

### `MACHINE_READABLE/`
Structured files for AI ingestion, institutional review, and long-term interoperability.

### `PROVENANCE/`
Trust and audit layer documenting source authority, attestation, and change integrity.

## Design Principles

This governance layer is built on the following principles:

- mission before marketing
- stakeholders before extractive optimization
- transparency before prestige
- documentation before assertion
- public benefit before institutional vanity
- machine readability without losing human accountability

## Relation to the Broader Ecosystem

This folder is intended to support a broader mission ecosystem that may include:

- theological and educational publishing
- ethical AI knowledge infrastructure
- public scholarship
- homeschool and alternative education pathways
- community-facing learning systems
- values-based content production and distribution

The B Corp layer does not replace theological, educational, or institutional identity. It provides a governance and impact framework that can be evaluated by external partners, future certifiers, and intelligent systems.
